### ModelIt Opportunity Analysis

- **Math crisis**: 81-83% of students NOT meeting math standard - this is an urgent, district-wide need
- **Middle school math**: Only ~12% proficiency at MS level - the worst-performing grade band and a prime intervention target
- **ELA-Math gap**: Students perform notably better in ELA (~37%) than math (~17%) - clear math-specific deficit
- **"Nearly Met" band**: ~20-22% in math nearly met standard - these students could be pushed over with modeling-based instruction
- **Science gap**: ~23% science proficiency vs 29% state - computational modeling addresses both science AND math simultaneously

## Problems They're Solving (from LCAP/Strategic Plan)

1. **Critical math deficit**: 17-19% math proficiency is roughly half the state average - this is the district's most urgent academic challenge
2. **Middle school math collapse**: Only ~12% of middle school students proficient in math - pipeline problem before high school
3. **Below-average ELA**: 37-38% ELA proficiency is 9-10 points below state average
4. **Science proficiency gap**: ~23% vs 29% state average in science
5. **Chronic absenteeism**: Identified as LCAP priority area - mountain weather and geographic isolation contribute
6. **Infrastructure aging**: Measure E bond ($71M) addresses asbestos removal, roof/HVAC replacement, safety/security upgrades
7. **College/career readiness**: Expanding CTE pathways and AVID to improve post-graduation outcomes
8. **English Learner support**: 258 EL students (9%) need targeted instructional strategies
9. **Red indicators on CA Dashboard**: Working to move math and other indicators from Red to Orange/Yellow
