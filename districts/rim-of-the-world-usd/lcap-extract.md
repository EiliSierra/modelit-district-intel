**Recommended Primary Pathway**: LCFF Supplemental/Concentration ($5.3M pool) for math intervention, combined with Foundation grant for pilot startup costs.

**Pricing Strategy for K-12 District**:
- K-12 district with ~2,877 students provides meaningful deal size
- Pilot at MS level (~500 students at Henck Intermediate) or HS level (~800 students at Rim HS)
- Initial pilot: $10-20K range (per-building license)
- District-wide expansion: $30-50K annually
- Measure E bond creates long-term STEM investment context

## Measure E Bond - Special Analysis

The $71M Measure E bond passed November 2024 with 60.93% approval. This is significant for ModelIt because:

| Bond Priority | Relevance |
|--------------|-----------|
| Safety and security improvements | LOW |
| Roof and HVAC replacement | LOW |
| Asbestos removal | LOW |
| Classroom upgrades | MEDIUM - upgraded classrooms need upgraded instructional tools |
| **Facilities for college/career readiness in science, math, engineering, arts, and skilled trades** | **HIGH - ModelIt is a tool FOR these facilities** |

**Key Insight**: Bond language explicitly mentions "college/career readiness in science, math, engineering" - ModelIt can be positioned as the instructional tool that makes new STEM facilities effective. Hardware without software is an empty room.
