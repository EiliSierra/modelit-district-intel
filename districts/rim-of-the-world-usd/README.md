## Why Rim of the World

1. **Critical math gap**: 17-19% math proficiency is half the state average - urgent intervention need
2. **$71M Measure E bond**: Capital flowing for STEM facilities, college/career readiness in science, math, engineering, arts, and skilled trades
3. **Mountain ecosystem = natural NGSS lab**: San Bernardino National Forest, Lake Arrowhead, Lake Gregory, fire ecology, watershed, wildlife
4. **Renewable energy pioneer**: First CA school with both solar AND wind turbines (vertical axis) - used as curriculum tools
5. **Board champion**: Jordana Ridland is a former math teacher with MA in Ed Tech - she understands the product
6. **Strong CTE pipeline**: Fire Science, Construction, Automotive, TV-Video Production - modeling connects to real-world applications
7. **AVID success**: 100% of AVID graduates accepted to 4-year universities - college readiness infrastructure exists
8. **Rim Educational Foundation**: Active 501(c)(3) funding STEM/CTE since 1983 - alternate funding pathway
