## Vendor Landscape Analysis

### Where ModelIt Fits

| Position | Assessment |
|----------|-----------|
| **Direct competitors present** | Unknown — likely minimal given mountain isolation |
| **Complementary tools** | Renewable energy curriculum + CTE + AVID → ModelIt adds computational layer |
| **Gap ModelIt fills** | Computational modeling for math reasoning — no current tool does this |
| **Price sensitivity** | MEDIUM — $71M bond brings capital, but instructional budgets are separate |

### Warm Intro Paths

1. **Rim Educational Foundation**: Active STEM/CTE funder since 1983. Position ModelIt as a CTE/STEM tool the Foundation can support.
2. **Jordana Ridland (Board)**: Former math teacher with MA in Ed Tech from APU. She understands the product space personally.
3. **Stephanie Phillips (CTE)**: 30+ years at Rim, oversees all CTE. ModelIt fits Fire Science and Construction pathways.
4. **Renewable Energy Connection**: Solar + wind turbine data can feed directly into ModelIt energy models.

### Competitive Threats

| Threat | Level | Rationale |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Status quo (no new tools) | MEDIUM | New superintendent is infrastructure-focused (Measure E), but math crisis demands action |
| Budget consumed by Measure E | LOW | Bond is capital (facilities); ModelIt is instructional (different budget line) |
| Free alternatives (PhET, etc.) | LOW | Free sims don't offer model-building |
| Geographic isolation limiting vendor interest | LOW for ModelIt | Virtual support + 1-2 annual visits is sufficient |
