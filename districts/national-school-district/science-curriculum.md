## ModelIt Positioning

### Why ModelIt Fills a Gap
1. **No confirmed science vendor** = no incumbent to displace
2. **WSSL partnership** proves district values external STEM partners — ModelIt is a natural addition
3. **17% CAST proficiency** demands intervention — ModelIt addresses NGSS SEP 2 (Developing and Using Models) and SEP 5 (Using Mathematics)
4. **54% EL population** — bilingual interface is a rare differentiator vs. any competitor
5. **1:1 iPad environment** — ModelIt must confirm iPad/web compatibility (CRITICAL)

### Recommended Positioning
- **Complement, don't compete**: Position ModelIt alongside WSSL as the computational modeling tool that extends Fleet Science Center's hands-on activities into digital practice
- **EL angle first**: Lead with bilingual interface for 54% EL population
- **NGSS Practice alignment**: Frame around SEP 2 + SEP 5 specifically
