## Key Talking Points

1. **54% English Learner population** — "More than half your students are English Learners, and only 10.87% meet math standards. ModelIt's bilingual interface was built for exactly this — students build and annotate scientific models in English or Spanish."

2. **NGSS Practice alignment** — "ModelIt directly addresses NGSS Science and Engineering Practice 2 (Developing and Using Models) and Practice 5 (Using Mathematics and Computational Thinking) — the two practices most districts struggle to implement."

3. **Navy STEM partnership extension** — "Your WSSL partnership with Fleet Science Center proves National City values STEM. ModelIt is the computational layer — students go from hands-on WSSL workshops to building digital models on their iPads."

4. **Cost-effective** — "We know you're managing a tight budget. The pilot is free for 6 weeks. If it works, we're talking $5-10K from existing Title III or LCFF funds — not a new line item."

5. **Sweetwater alignment** — "Sweetwater UHSD is also in our pipeline. A K-6 foundation in computational modeling at NSD means students arrive at Sweetwater with STEM skills already in place."
