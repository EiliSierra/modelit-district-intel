## Why This District — ModelIt Fit (7 Reasons)

1. **STEAM Labs Coming Online**: Measure HH ($265M bond) is installing STEAM Labs at 8 of 9 elementary schools — new lab spaces need aligned modeling tools that match the NGSS inquiry philosophy
2. **PLTW K-12 Articulated Pathway**: California's first district with K-12 STEM pathway (Calavera Hills ES → MS → Sage Creek HS). ModelIt's computational modeling extends PLTW engineering thinking — complements, doesn't compete
3. **Sage Creek HS PLTW Distinguished 2022**: Top engineering school in SD county; ModelIt adds systems modeling depth to PLTW biomedical and engineering capstone projects
4. **EL Science Gap = 19.2%**: Even in a high-performing district, English Learners score 39 points below district average in science. ModelIt's bilingual (English/Spanish) interface directly addresses this underserved subgroup
5. **New Superintendent = Fresh Start**: Dr. Andrea Norman started June 2025; stated priorities are "bonds, budgets, and curriculum" — open window for curriculum conversations with a new leader who has a curriculum director background
6. **NGSS Full Implementation**: District is NGSS-aligned district-wide; ModelIt's SEP 2 (Developing and Using Models) and SEP 5 (Computational Thinking) are core alignment hooks for teachers already trained in NGSS
7. **Active AI & EdTech Adoption**: District is already using Snorkl, MagicSchool, and planning Google Gemini — culture of piloting new ed-tech tools is established
