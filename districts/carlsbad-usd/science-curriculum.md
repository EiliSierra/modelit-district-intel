## What This Means for ModelIt

### The Opportunity
Carlsbad USD has an extensive STEM ecosystem but it is **enrichment-heavy and activity-based** (robotics competitions, science olympiad, STEAM labs, field trips). What's missing is a **systems-level modeling curriculum** that ties these experiences together with computational thinking.

ModelIt fills the gap between:
- "Hands-on lab activities" (what PLTW and CEF fund)
- "Building a conceptual model of a system" (what NGSS SEP-2 requires)
- "Using mathematics to analyze a model" (what NGSS SEP-5 requires)
