## What This Means for ModelIt — Positioning Strategy

### Core Message
ModelIt fills the **computational modeling gap** in Carlsbad's already-strong science ecosystem. It is not a curriculum replacement — it is the tool that makes NGSS Science & Engineering Practice 2 (Developing and Using Models) and Practice 5 (Using Mathematics and Computational Thinking) *actually teachable*. PLTW, STEAM Labs, and Science Days all assume students can build models — ModelIt is the tool that lets them do it.

### How ModelIt Complements Each Current Program

| Current Tool | What It Does | What ModelIt Adds | Pitch Message |
|-------------|-------------|------------------|---------------|
| PLTW Engineering (K-12) | Physical design, iteration, hardware build | Computational systems modeling before and during build — test parameters digitally | "ModelIt gives PLTW students the systems modeling tool their EDD capstone needs" |
| PLTW Biomedical Sciences | Biological content, lab investigations | Model cellular respiration, disease spread, genetic inheritance computationally | "Students model the biology before they run the lab — deeper understanding" |
| STEAM Labs | Physical hands-on tinkering | Model predictions and test hypotheses computationally alongside lab work | "Students predict, then experiment — modeling + making = deeper NGSS inquiry" |
| VEX Robotics | Physical robot programming | Systems behavior modeling — understand why the robot does what it does before building | "Model the system, then build it" |
| Science Days | Industry speakers, exploration | Follow-up computational modeling of observed systems — lagoon, farm, engineering | "After Science Day on water quality, students model the watershed" |
| Agua Hedionda Lagoon | Field ecosystem observation | Computational ecosystem modeling — simulate lagoon health variables | "Students who visit the lagoon can then model it — food webs, water quality" |
| FIRST Tech Challenge | Competitive robotics build | Systems design simulation — predict robot behavior before hardware commitment | "Iterate designs digitally before committing to hardware" |
| Science Olympiad | Competitive multi-discipline | Systems modeling for ecology, physics, and engineering events | "Model the science competition scenarios before the event" |
| Coastal Roots Farm | Field agriculture learning | Model crop systems, water use, food chains computationally | "From farm observation to ecosystem model — systems thinking" |
