## Key Talking Points

1. **"Students BUILD models, not just run them."** — ModelIt is the only K–12 tool where students construct their own systems models from scratch. PhET, Gizmos, Discovery Education — students interact with someone else's model. ModelIt develops the modeling competency NGSS SEP-2 actually requires.

2. **"It bridges your STEAM labs and your PLTW programs."** — Carlsbad has physical STEAM labs (Measure HH) and PLTW engineering pathways. ModelIt is the software layer that ties hands-on experimentation to computational modeling — the exact workflow that NGSS intends.

3. **"Your EL students need this."** — English Learner students in Carlsbad score only 19.2% proficient in science and 27.58% in math — 39 and 34 points below the district average. ModelIt is fully bilingual (English/Spanish). The visual, model-building interface reduces language barriers for math and science concept development.

4. **"It's complementary, not competing."** — ModelIt doesn't replace your science textbook, your PLTW courses, or your STEAM lab activities. It adds the computational modeling practice that every NGSS-aligned district needs but most tools don't provide.

5. **"Agua Hedionda Lagoon is a natural ModelIt lesson."** — Grade 7 students collect real lagoon water data. ModelIt is where they build a system model of WHY the lagoon behaves that way — and predict what happens under different conditions. Real data → computational model → predictions. That's NGSS in action.

6. **"NSF SBIR Phase II–funded credibility."** — ModelIt has been peer-reviewed and funded by NSF's most rigorous review process for educational innovation. It's not a startup experiment — it has a research base.

7. **"Pilot with zero risk."** — We offer a free 4–6 week pilot for 2–3 teachers before any purchase decision. No IT integration required (runs in Chrome on your existing Chromebooks). We provide all teacher PD. You evaluate with real students before committing.
