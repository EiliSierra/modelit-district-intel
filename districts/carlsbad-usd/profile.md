## STEM Landscape

### K-12 STEM Pathway — "STEM Street"
Carlsbad Unified was recognized as **California's first district with an articulated K-12 STEM pathway**, anchored on College Boulevard. The pipeline runs: Calavera Hills Elementary → Calavera Hills Middle School → Sage Creek High School.

### Elementary STEM Programs (TK–5)

|---------|---------|-------------|---------|

## Key Budget Context (Problems the District is Solving)

Based on LCAP goals and public reporting:
1. **EL Academic Gap**: English Learners scoring 30–40 pts below district average in all subjects — equity imperative
2. **Middle/HS Math Dip**: Proficiency drops from 75–81% (Grades 3–4) to 47–56% (Grades 7–11) — need engagement tools
3. **Budget Deficit Management**: $7.5M deficit means new tools must demonstrate ROI or access non-general-fund dollars
4. **STEAM Lab Activation**: New physical spaces being built need instructional programs to fill them
5. **NGSS Deep Implementation**: Beyond standards alignment to true inquiry and modeling practices
6. **AI Integration**: Active exploration of AI tools with new board policy — innovative culture
