## Decision Process

Based on Carlsbad USD's structure, curriculum decisions follow this path:

1. **Science TOSA (Ashley Crawford)** evaluates and recommends tool
2. **Director of Elementary or Secondary Ed** (Fuentes or Ecker) reviews with TOSA
3. **Asst. Superintendent Instructional Services** (Brockett) approves
4. **Superintendent** (Norman) authorizes if above site budget threshold
5. **Board of Education** approves if contract exceeds ~$10K-25K (verify threshold)

For a site-level pilot (<$10K, site discretionary), only school principal + TOSA approval needed.

## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Budget deficit limits discretionary spending | **High** | High | Target non-general-fund sources: Measure HH bond + Title IV-A; keep pilot under $5K |
| Ashley Crawford email is wrong / she's not the contact | Medium | Medium | Call to verify before sending; have backup in Bryan Brockett |
| District is satisfied with PLTW and sees no gap | Medium | Medium | Reframe: ModelIt is NOT a PLTW replacement — it's the computational modeling layer PLTW lacks |
| "We already tried [tool X]" objection | Low | Medium | Understand what they tried and why it didn't work; map how ModelIt is different (student-built vs. teacher-led) |
| Science curriculum adoption is due and they want to wait | Low-Medium | Medium | Supplemental framing avoids the adoption cycle — ModelIt doesn't replace core curriculum |
| New superintendent in discovery mode — not ready to decide | Medium | Low | Dr. Norman has curriculum director background; she's more likely to engage than a business-background supt. |
| Gatekeeper blocks access to Ashley Crawford | Low | Medium | Use Executive Assistant to schedule; alternatively approach via CEdF or SDCOE connection |
| School year timing — no budget decisions in summer | Low | Medium | Plan Phase 3-4 for April-May; avoid June-July; re-engage August after school year starts |
| Competition from free tools (PhET) | Medium | Low | PhET = interact with simulations; ModelIt = BUILD simulations. Position as higher-order cognitive tool |
| CEdF grant cycle doesn't align | Low | Low | Use as supplementary path, not primary; don't depend on CEdF as sole pathway |
