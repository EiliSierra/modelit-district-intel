## The Hook

Carlsbad Unified has built world-class STEM infrastructure — STEAM labs at 5 elementary schools, PLTW pathways from middle through high school, Science Olympiad funded by Viasat — but the bridge from hands-on activity to systems-level computational thinking is missing. ModelIt gives CUSD students exactly what NGSS Science & Engineering Practice 2 requires: **the ability to BUILD and run their own system models**, not just interact with pre-built simulations. With English Learner students scoring only 19.2% proficient in science (a 39-point gap vs. the district overall), and with a new superintendent prioritizing UDL and MTSS, ModelIt's bilingual modeling platform is the scientifically rigorous equity tool that Carlsbad's STEAM infrastructure is waiting for.

3. **Tech approval** — Robert Allen approves via CA National Data Privacy Agreement (NDPA) for student data
4. **Budget commitment** — Bryan Brockett approves purchase/pilot funding from instructional materials or LCAP action
5. **Superintendent awareness** — Dr. Norman notified (not a gatekeeper at pilot stage; more relevant for expansion)
6. **Board approval** — Only if contract exceeds bid threshold (~$15K typically triggers board consent calendar item)

### Phase 5: District Contract (2026–27)
