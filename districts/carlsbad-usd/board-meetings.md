## Purchasing & Warrants Section

*This section tracks vendor payments, purchase orders, and contracts from board consent calendars. Extraction from board minutes PDFs required.*

### Vendor Payments Extraction Template
When reviewing board minutes PDFs, flag items in these categories:

| Category | What to Look For | Why It Matters |
|----------|-----------------|----------------|
| Curriculum Publisher Payments | "Adoption," "Instructional Materials," publisher name POs | Reveals current science/math publishers — key for competitive analysis |
| EdTech Platform Costs | Renewal agreements, SaaS contracts (Renaissance, Achieve3000, etc.) | Shows budget allocation and contract timing |
| Technology Hardware | Chromebook orders, STEAM lab equipment, Measure HH purchases | Shows device refresh cycle and lab equipment vendors |
| PD Provider Payments | Professional development contracts, conference registration | Reveals who trains teachers — PD partnership opportunity |
| STEM Equipment Orders | Science lab supplies, VEX robotics, LEGO kits | Shows STEM program spending scale |
| Assessment Platform Costs | STAR, DIBELS renewal agreements | Confirms active vendors and contract sizes |
| New Vendor Approvals | "Approval to enter agreement with [vendor]" | New EdTech tools being piloted |
