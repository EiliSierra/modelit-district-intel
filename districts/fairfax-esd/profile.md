## Superintendent Profile

**Lora Brown** — Superintendent
- **Phone:** (661) 366-7221

- **Tenure:** July 2022 (interim) → permanent appointment thereafter

## Problems They're Solving (Inferred from LCAP / Public Records)

1. **Chronically low math and science proficiency** — Math at 19.7%, science at 10.7%; below state averages at every grade level
2. **EL academic equity crisis** — ~903 EL students with 1.72% science and 1.6% math proficiency
3. **Post-FCMAT accountability** — Rebuilding board and community trust after governance scandal
4. **Science curriculum gap** — No confirmed core science program; NGSS transition incomplete
5. **Teacher quality and retention** — High-need southeast Bakersfield community; PDG and coaching needs
6. **Instructional coherence** — Decentralized STEM activity (Mastery Coding at one school, STEM teacher at another) without district-wide framework
