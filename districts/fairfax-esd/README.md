## Why This District Fits ModelIt

1. **Science white space** — CAST at 10.7% (19 pts below state avg); no confirmed science publisher at any grade level — pure greenfield opportunity.
2. **EL science gap near zero** — EL students at 1.72% CAST; ModelIt's bilingual English/Spanish platform directly addresses this equity crisis and is a core LCAP priority.
3. **Math proficiency collapsed at every grade** — Grade 3 starts at 26.33%, drops to 14.47% by Grade 8; ModelIt's SEP-5 computational thinking hook supports cross-curricular math intervention.
4. **Clever SSO deployed** — Zero IT lift for ModelIt; instant single-sign-on for all students day one.
5. **LCAP equity spending** — District qualifies for LCFF Supplemental/Concentration; Title IV-A available for STEM; ~$48M revenue base.
6. **Fairfax Junior High is the ideal pilot school** — Grades 7–8, 11% math proficiency, dedicated science teachers, principal Ana Perez; maximum impact per dollar.
7. **Dedicated STEM teacher at Zephyr Lane** — Lauren Saavedra confirmed STEM Teacher; instant champion for a classroom pilot.
8. **Mastery Coding precedent** — District already piloted and adopted coding curriculum at Shirley Lane; shows appetite for EdTech innovation.
9. **Post-FCMAT stabilization** — Under Lora Brown's steady leadership since 2022, district is in active recovery mode and receptive to programs with measurable ROI.
