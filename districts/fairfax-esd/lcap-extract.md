## LCAP-Stated Problems (from Goals section)

1. **Low academic achievement**: Math 19.7%, ELA 28.13%, Science 10.7% — all far below state averages
2. **EL academic gap**: English Learners have 1.6% math, 3.28% ELA, 1.72% science proficiency — near-zero performance
3. **SED equity**: 97% socioeconomically disadvantaged (confirmed) — entire district is high-need
4. **Teacher quality and retention**: Southeast Bakersfield recruiting challenges; high-need student population
5. **Infrastructure gaps**: Virginia Avenue Elementary needs full modernization; network infrastructure aging
6. **Early literacy foundation**: 47% of students Well Below Benchmark on Acadience (transitioning to DIBELS)
