## Why Bear Valley USD

1. **Math crisis**: 30.35% overall, ~18% at HS, 45% at Level 1 — math is the acute need
2. **BEARTECH program**: Nature-based, place-based STEM already in place — ModelIt adds the computational layer
3. **Board President PhD Physics**: Dr. Foulkes has a PhD in Experimental Particle Physics from UC Riverside — he understands computational modeling at a deep personal level
4. **Mountain ecosystem laboratory**: Big Bear Lake, Pebble Plain (7th most biodiverse place in US), San Bernardino National Forest = NGSS phenomena goldmine
5. **213-acre outdoor ed property**: Donated to district — a living laboratory for computational science
6. **Big Bear Solar Observatory**: World-class NJIT research facility in the community — solar modeling
7. **Below-median funding**: Grant-sensitive district, position subsidized pilots and grants
8. **"Educate, Inspire, Prepare"**: Maps directly to ModelIt's mission
9. **Small district, insider superintendent**: 24-year BVUSD veteran, tight community, accessible decision-making
