# Bear Valley Unified School District - Vendors & Partners

**Last Updated**: 2026-02-10
**District Size**: ~2,132 students, 6 schools (K-12)

> **Mountain Community Note**: Bear Valley USD serves the Big Bear Lake area in the San Bernardino Mountains at 6,750+ ft elevation. The community is tightly knit with strong education trust and foundation involvement. CEP (Community Eligibility Provision) district — free meals for ALL students.

## Vendor Landscape Analysis

### Where ModelIt Fits

| Position | Assessment |
|----------|-----------|
| **Direct competitors present** | Unknown — likely minimal in a small mountain district |
| **Complementary tools** | BEARTECH (hands-on nature science) + Solar Observatory + Pebble Plain → ModelIt adds computational layer |
| **Gap ModelIt fills** | Computational modeling for math reasoning — BEARTECH does observation, ModelIt does computation |
| **Price sensitivity** | HIGH — below-median per-pupil funding ($2,315 below state median). Position with grants/subsidized pricing |

### Warm Intro Paths

1. **Dr. Stephen Foulkes (Board President)**: PhD in Experimental Particle Physics from UC Riverside. He understands computational modeling at a personal, professional level. The warmest possible intro.
2. **Big Bear Valley Education Trust**: Funds BEARTECH and STEMQUEST. ModelIt is the computational modeling layer that makes BEARTECH more rigorous. Natural co-funding partner.
3. **BVUSD Education Foundation**: Donated 213 acres. If they fund land, they may fund software that uses the land for data.
4. **Big Bear Solar Observatory (NJIT)**: Solar modeling is a direct ModelIt use case. Observatory scientists could validate models.

### Competitive Threats

| Threat | Level | Rationale |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Status quo (BEARTECH is enough) | MEDIUM | BEARTECH is observation-based; ModelIt adds computation. Different, not competing. |
| Budget constraints | HIGH | Below-median funding. Must position with grants or subsidized pricing. |
| Free alternatives (PhET, etc.) | LOW | Free sims don't offer model-building |
| Geographic isolation | LOW for ModelIt | Virtual support + 1-2 annual visits sufficient |
| STEMQUEST museum consuming STEM budget | LOW | Museum is multi-year; ModelIt is immediate and classroom-based |
