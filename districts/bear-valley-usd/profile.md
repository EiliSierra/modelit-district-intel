### ModelIt Opportunity Analysis

- **Math**: 69.65% of students NOT meeting math standard — nearly 7 in 10 below proficiency
- **Level 1 crisis**: 45% of students at Level 1 in math (Standard Not Met)
- **HS math collapse**: Only ~18% proficient at high school — catastrophic drop from elementary (~46%)
- **"Nearly Met" band**: 25% in math are close — computational modeling can push these students over the threshold
- **ELA-Math gap**: Students perform significantly better in ELA (42.56%) than math (30.35%) — math is the acute need
- **SED gap**: Economically disadvantaged students 7+ points below district average in both subjects
- **Science at 28%**: Below state average, reinforcing need for NGSS-aligned science instruction

### Notable STEM Infrastructure
- **BEARTECH program** — Nature-based, place-based science curriculum leveraging Big Bear's mountain ecology. Expanding district-wide.
- **213-acre outdoor education property** — Donated by BVUSD Education Foundation. Includes trails and youth sports complex.
- **Big Bear Solar Observatory** — World-class NJIT research facility in the community, available for student engagement.
- **Pebble Plain Ecological Reserve** — ~400 acres, 7th most biodiverse place in the US. Connected to BEARTECH and Ed Trust.
- **Big Bear Discovery Center** — US Forest Service interpretive center for environmental education.
- **Camp Whittle / Naturalists at Large** — Environmental education programs at 7,200 ft elevation.
- **Positive Adventures** — Outdoor education provider partnered with the district.

## Problems They're Solving

1. **Math proficiency crisis**: 30.35% overall, ~18% at high school — massive HS collapse
2. **Below-median funding**: Per-pupil revenue $2,315 below state median = need to stretch every dollar
3. **BEARTECH expansion**: Scaling nature-based STEM from one school to district-wide
4. **Mountain community isolation**: Students in remote mountain community need career-relevant STEM exposure
5. **HS math collapse**: 46% proficiency at elementary drops to 18% at high school — pipeline problem
6. **EL support**: ~290 English Learners need language-accessible instruction
7. **STEMQUEST museum initiative**: Ed Trust planning hands-on STEM museum — computational modeling fits
