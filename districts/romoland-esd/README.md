## Why Romoland ESD

1. **Significant math gap**: 15.3% math proficiency — 19 points below state average; urgent intervention needed
2. **High-needs population**: 92.4% economically disadvantaged + 29.7% EL = Title I + Title III eligible
3. **K-8 sweet spot**: Grades 5-8 are ModelIt's strongest fit; this district IS grades K-8
4. **Suburban growth district**: Growing enrollment (4,064 → 4,800) indicates community investment
5. **Bilingual imperative**: 72.2% Hispanic + 29.7% EL = ModelIt's bilingual EN/ES is differentiator
6. **Elementary focus**: No high school distraction; district can focus resources on K-8 improvement
7. **Riverside County proximity**: Close to Carlsbad for in-person demo and support
