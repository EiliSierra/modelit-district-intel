## 5-Phase Approach

### Phase 1: Warm Outreach (March 2026)
**Actions:**
- Send personalized email to Tammy Barrera (Asst. Supt.) referencing Beardsley's specific CAASPP data and PLTW connection
- Simultaneously email Mac Robertson with a direct PLTW-complement message
- Email Ryan Frank with a brief Clever integration note
- **Goal:** Get a response from at least one of the three; aim for a 20-minute call

### Phase 2: Demo & Discovery (April 2026)
**Actions:**
- Deliver a 30-minute virtual or in-person demo to Robertson + West (BJHS team)
- Show: Build-It interface → Run-It simulation → Student output → Teacher dashboard
- Ask Robertson to identify one unit where ModelIt fits (e.g., a Design & Modeling or Medical Detectives unit)
- Offer free pilot: "We'll set up your classroom in Clever — no IT work required"
- **Goal:** Confirm pilot school (BJHS) and pilot teacher (Robertson + one science teacher)

### Phase 3: Free Pilot (May–June 2026)
**Actions:**
- Launch 6-week free pilot in 1-2 Robertson/Parker/Bush class sections at BJHS
- Provide onboarding PD (1 hour) and weekly check-ins
- Collect usage data, student work samples, teacher feedback
- At end of pilot: produce a 2-page pilot summary report with CAST-readiness framing
- **Goal:** Documented pilot evidence for Barrera/Miller; LCAP inclusion discussion

### Phase 4: District Buy-In (June–July 2026)
**Actions:**
- Present pilot results to Barrera and Miller
- Show LCAP alignment: "This addresses Goal 1 — academic achievement for SED students — through NGSS SEP-2 modeling"
- Provide budget brief: "Title IV-A or LCAP supplemental funds can cover $15-20K district-wide license for BJHS + Grade 5"
- **Goal:** Verbal commitment; draft LCAP action language for 2026-27

### Phase 5: Contract & Expansion (July–August 2026)
**Actions:**
- Submit formal proposal with pricing: BJHS pilot ($8-12K) → optional Grade 5 expansion ($5-8K)
- Rhonda Phinney (CBO) reviews and identifies budget line
- Paul Miller approves; board consent calendar item if above threshold
- Teacher PD scheduled for August before school year
- **Goal:** Signed contract; implementation start September 2026
