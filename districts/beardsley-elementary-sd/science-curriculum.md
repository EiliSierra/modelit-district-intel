## What This Means for ModelIt

### The Opportunity
Beardsley has **no confirmed K-8 science curriculum publisher** — complete greenfield at the elementary level. At the junior high, PLTW already establishes a modeling mindset. ModelIt extends this into science class so Mac Robertson and his colleagues (Parker, Bush) can address NGSS SEP-2 together across PLTW and core science.

| Existing Tool | ModelIt's Relationship | Key Message |
