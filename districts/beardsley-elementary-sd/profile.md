## Problems They Are Solving

1. Chronically low math proficiency (13.29% overall; 8.47% in 7th grade)
2. Science proficiency crisis (11.37% vs. 29% state average)
3. ELA literacy gap (22.87% vs. 47% state average)
4. Staffing shortages (5 bus driver vacancies in 2024-25; teacher recruitment)
5. Parent engagement (Fresno State partnership for family digital/financial literacy)
