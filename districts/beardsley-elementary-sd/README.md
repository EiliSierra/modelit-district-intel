## Why This District (7 Reasons ModelIt Fits)

1. **Critical math gap**: 13.29% overall math proficiency — 20+ points below state average — signals urgent need for engaging, visual intervention tools.
2. **PLTW already at BJHS**: Mac Robertson teaches PLTW Design and Modeling at Beardsley Junior High, meaning a modeling mindset already exists. ModelIt is the natural science complement.
3. **7th grade math cliff**: Only 8.47% of 7th graders meet math standards — the steepest grade-level drop. ModelIt's math-science integration directly addresses this.
4. **Green field for science vendors**: No confirmed K-8 science curriculum publisher; no Amplify, FOSS, or other incumbent. ModelIt enters a white space.
5. **Bilingual platform fit**: 63.6% Hispanic enrollment; Clever SSO already in place for easy student login. ModelIt's Spanish/English bilingual interface is a direct fit.
6. **NGSS modeling gap**: 11.37% CAST science (vs 29% state average) — district cannot currently achieve SEP-2 (Developing and Using Models) at scale.
