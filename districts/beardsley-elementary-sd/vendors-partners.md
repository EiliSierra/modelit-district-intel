| Threat | Scenario | ModelIt Response |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| Amplify Science adopted as K-8 anchor | District enters curriculum adoption cycle | "ModelIt adds NGSS SEP-2 modeling that Amplify itself doesn't fully address. Complementary, not competing." |
| PLTW elementary (Launch) expansion | District expands PLTW to K-6 | "PLTW Launch is engineering design. ModelIt is scientific modeling. Different practices, different value." |
| Budget pressure → free tools (PhET) | Small district tight budget | "Free tools are consumptive — students interact but don't create. ModelIt makes students authors; SEP-2 requires developing models, not using them." |
