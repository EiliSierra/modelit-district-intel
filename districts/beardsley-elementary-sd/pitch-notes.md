## Key Talking Points

1. **The Math Cliff is Real — and ModelIt Addresses the Root Cause**
   > "Beardsley's 7th graders scored 8.47% in math — the lowest grade-level score in the district. That's not a computation problem — it's an application problem. Students who only practice math in isolation struggle to transfer it. ModelIt gives them math with a purpose: building and testing scientific models where variables, ratios, and rates are the point."

2. **PLTW Already Opened the Door**
   > "The fact that PLTW is active at BJHS tells us your students already understand systems thinking at the engineering level. ModelIt brings that same mindset into science class. Together, PLTW and ModelIt give students the most complete modeling education available in K-8."

3. **Greenfield Science = Zero Conflict**
   > "We couldn't find a confirmed K-8 science publisher for Beardsley. That means we're not asking you to replace anything. ModelIt fills a gap — the computational modeling gap — that NGSS explicitly requires and that no existing tool in your district currently addresses."

4. **Bilingual from Day One**
   > "With 63.6% Hispanic enrollment, a bilingual platform isn't a nice-to-have — it's essential. ModelIt runs in English and Spanish, and teachers can toggle between languages in any lesson. Your EL students engage with the same rigorous content, in the language where they learn best."

5. **Clever SSO = Zero IT Lift**
   > "Clever is already deployed at Beardsley. That means every student already has a ModelIt login. The typical technology barrier — provisioning accounts, managing passwords, training on a new system — doesn't exist here. We can have a class running within 48 hours of your go-ahead."

6. **NSF Credibility + Bilingual = LCAP Alignment**
   > "ModelIt is NSF SBIR Phase II funded — the federal government's highest vote of confidence for an early-stage education technology company. That credibility matters when you're writing LCAP documentation and justifying a new program to your board. And bilingual delivery directly supports your English Learner metrics."

7. **MicroMayhem: Science Learning That Feels Like Gaming**
   > "We know engagement is a challenge when 87% of your students aren't meeting science standards. MicroMayhem is a game where students battle real biological threats using the same modeling skills they learn in class. Students love it because it feels like a video game. Teachers love it because every move reinforces real science."

8. **Free Pilot First — Data Before Dollars**
   > "We don't ask districts to buy before they've seen results. Run a free 6-week pilot at BJHS. At the end, we provide a usage report, student work samples, and a CAST-readiness summary. You get the evidence you need to make an LCAP case — before you spend a dollar."
