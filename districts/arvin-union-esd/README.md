## Why This District — ModelIt Fit (7 Reasons)

1. **Critical science gap**: 15.71% CAST proficiency = bottom quartile in California; ~59% estimated "Nearly Met" cohort = large, movable target audience
2. **No science vendor confirmed**: Zero incumbent to displace; no existing science curriculum investment; complete white space — the rarest situation in K-12 EdTech
3. **Zero integration friction**: Canvas LMS (arvinschools.instructure.com) + Clever SSO + Chromebooks already in place; ModelIt needs no new login or IT work
4. **Maximum equity funding**: ~94% FRPM eligibility → maximum LCFF Supplemental + Title I; Title IV-A (SSAE) is the primary ModelIt funding pathway
5. **Bilingual platform match**: 15.5% EL students + Dual Immersion program + 92.8% Hispanic community; ModelIt is the only NGSS simulation platform with bilingual English/Spanish interface
6. **AVID schoolwide**: Students trained in academic habits, note-taking, and inquiry — directly prepares them to engage with modeling tools
7. **Small district = fast cycle**: 4 schools, 2,963 students → pilot results visible within one semester; board approval likely at a single meeting
