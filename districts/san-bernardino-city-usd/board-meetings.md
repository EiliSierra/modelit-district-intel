## Extraction Template for PDF Review

When reviewing board meeting agendas/minutes, look for:

### Curriculum & Instruction Items
- [ ] Science curriculum adoption/renewal contracts
- [ ] Math curriculum or intervention tool purchases
- [ ] EdTech platform approvals or renewals
- [ ] STEM program expansions
- [ ] Professional development contracts
- [ ] CTE pathway approvals

### Budget Items
- [ ] LCAP approval (annual, typically June)
- [ ] Technology bond measures
- [ ] Title I/II/IV allocations
- [ ] Supplemental/Concentration grant spending
- [ ] Vendor contracts over $50K

### Personnel Items
- [ ] STEM coordinator/specialist hiring
- [ ] Science/Math department leadership changes
- [ ] Ed Tech staffing

### Partnership Items
- [ ] University partnership agreements
- [ ] Industry/nonprofit STEM partnerships
- [ ] Grant awards (NSF, state, foundation)
