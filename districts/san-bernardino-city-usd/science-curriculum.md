## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

ModelIt should be positioned as a **supplemental computational modeling tool** that:

1. **Complements** whatever core science curriculum they use (FOSS, Amplify, etc.) -- does NOT replace
2. **Fills the modeling gap** -- NGSS SEP 2 (Developing and Using Models) and SEP 5 (Using Mathematics and Computational Thinking) are requirements, but no tool currently addresses them
3. **Bridges science and math** -- unique cross-curricular value in a district with low proficiency in both
4. **Supports CAST preparation** -- California Science Test includes items requiring modeling understanding
5. **Enhances CTE pathways** -- engineering modeling at San Bernardino HS, biomedical modeling at Pacific HS

### How ModelIt Complements Current Tools

| Current Tool | ModelIt Complement |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Core science curriculum (FOSS/Amplify/TCI) | Adds computational modeling layer that textbooks lack |
| PLTW Biomedical (Pacific HS) | Adds systems modeling to biomedical investigations |
| IB Science (Arroyo Valley, Cajon) | Advanced modeling for IB Internal Assessments |
| AP Science | Modeling component for AP labs and investigations |
| Flinn Scientific (lab safety) | Not competing -- different category entirely |
| Math curriculum (Integrated Math) | Cross-curricular modeling connects math concepts to science applications |
