## Problems They're Solving (from LCAP/District Structure)

1. **Low math proficiency** (~15-22% vs 34% state avg) -- massive gap to close
2. **Low science proficiency** (~10-18% vs 30% state avg) -- even larger gap
3. **Equity gaps** for African American students -- specific math achievement programs at grades 4, 5, 8, IM1
4. **English Learner support** -- large EL population (~25-35%), Multilingual Programs department
5. **College/career readiness** -- Linked Learning, CTE pathways, dual enrollment expansion
6. **Strategic transformation** -- Vision 2030: SBCUSD SHINES driving innovation
