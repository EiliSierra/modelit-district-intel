## The Hook

> "SBCUSD's S.T.E.A.M. Department is one of the few dedicated STEAM offices in the Inland Empire -- which tells us you're serious about STEM innovation. ModelIt is an NSF-funded computational modeling platform that gives students the tools to build, simulate, and analyze their own systems models, directly addressing NGSS Practices 2 and 5. With Superintendent Arellano's Vision 2030 and his C-STEM leadership background, we believe SBCUSD is the ideal partner to bring research-grade modeling into the classroom -- starting with a pilot at Del Vallejo STEAM Academy."

| # | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |

| 3 | Competing priorities (Vision 2030 has many initiatives) | MEDIUM | MEDIUM | Align ModelIt WITH Vision 2030, not as separate initiative |
| 4 | Platform fatigue ("another tool") | MEDIUM | MEDIUM | Position as filling a gap, not adding a tool. "You have everything except modeling" |

| 6 | Research Request process delays | MEDIUM | MEDIUM | Submit research request early. Have compliance docs ready |
