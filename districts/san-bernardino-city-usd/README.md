## Why San Bernardino City USD

1. **Massive math gap**: ~15-22% proficiency vs 34% state average (12-19 pts below)
2. **C-STEM Superintendent**: Mauricio Arellano won UC Davis C-STEM Superintendent of the Year 2020
3. **Dedicated S.T.E.A.M. Department**: Rare at district level -- shows institutional STEM commitment
4. **Del Vallejo STEAM Academy**: Middle school with STEAM in its name -- ideal pilot site
5. **PLTW at Pacific HS**: Already paying for premium STEM curriculum (Project Lead The Way Biomedical)
6. **Greenfield opportunity**: No computational modeling/simulation tools found anywhere in district
7. **Equity alignment**: ~88-92% Hispanic, ~85-90% low-income -- ModelIt's visual interface reduces barriers
8. **Vision 2030: SBCUSD SHINES**: Active strategic transformation -- open to innovative solutions
9. **Enormous scale**: ~49K students = high-value contract ($50K pilot -> $250K+ scale)
10. **STEM board members**: Felicia Alexander (Caltech/Aerospace), Dr. Wyatt (CTE/ROP), Danny Tillman (IT)
