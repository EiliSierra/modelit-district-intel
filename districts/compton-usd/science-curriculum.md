## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

ModelIt should be positioned as a **complementary computational modeling tool** that fills the gap between Compton's strong STEM programs (robotics, coding) and their critically low science proficiency (~14%).

**Key message**: "Your students are building robots and coding -- now give them the tool to BUILD scientific models and understand the systems behind the science. ModelIt lets students develop and use computational models aligned to NGSS Practice 2, which is the missing piece between hands-on STEM and conceptual science understanding."

### How ModelIt Complements Current Tools

| Current Tool | What It Does | What ModelIt Adds |
|-------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Robotics | Physical engineering, coding | Systems-level modeling of how robots interact with environments |
| Coding/CS | Programming logic | Computational thinking applied to scientific phenomena |
| eSports | Competition, teamwork | N/A (different purpose) |
| Maker Spaces | Physical prototyping | Digital modeling before/after building |
| NAF Academy | Career exploration | NGSS-aligned modeling for science courses |
