## Superintendent Profile

**Dr. Darin Brawley, Ed.D.**
- **Tenure**: 13 years (longest-serving active superintendent in Compton history)
- **Education**: Ed.D. Educational Leadership (USC), M.A. Educational Leadership (CSU San Bernardino), B.A. (University of Redlands)
- **Previous**: Superintendent at Adelanto Elementary SD (3 years); career path: HS teacher, MS assistant principal, elementary principal, MS principal, Director of HR, Executive Director of HR, Deputy Superintendent of Student Learning
- **Recognition**: LA County Superintendent of the Year 2019
- **Key Achievements**: Led district from fiscal instability to stability; schools earned National Blue Ribbon, Title I Performing Schools, CA Distinguished School awards; oversaw construction of new Compton High School campus
- **Priorities**: Data-driven instruction, equity, STEM/STEAM expansion, community partnerships

## Problems They're Solving (from LCAP/Strategic Plan)

1. Science proficiency crisis (~14% vs 29% state avg)
2. English Learner achievement gap (16% ELA proficiency)
3. Closing remaining equity gaps despite overall gains
4. Maintaining improvement momentum after #1 ranking in state test score gains
5. Expanding STEM access across all grade levels
