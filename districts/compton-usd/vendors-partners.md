## Vendor Landscape Analysis

### Where ModelIt Fits
- **Gap**: Despite robust technology partnerships (Apple, Google, Microsoft), the district lacks a dedicated computational modeling platform for NGSS science
- **Opportunity**: The vacancy in the Chief Academic Officer role means curriculum decisions may be open for new perspectives
- **Strength**: Existing 1:1 device infrastructure means ModelIt can deploy immediately

### Warm Introduction Paths
1. **Digital Promise** -- if ModelIt/Cell Collective has any Digital Promise relationship, this is a warm path
2. **NAF Network** -- NAF academy districts often share resources; check for NAF connections
3. **NSF SBIR credibility** -- Compton values research-backed programs; NSF funding is strong social proof

### Competitive Threats
- **Apple/Google ecosystem** -- district may prefer tools within existing ecosystems
- **Free alternatives** -- PhET, other free simulations may be used informally
- **Amplify Science** -- if adopted, Amplify's digital tools may satisfy some modeling needs
