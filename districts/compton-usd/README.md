## Why Compton

1. **Severe science gap**: ~14% science proficiency vs 29% state average -- massive need for STEM tools
2. **Strong STEM commitment**: NAF-recognized STEM Excellence across K-12, robotics, coding from preschool
3. **Equity alignment**: 82% Hispanic, 17% Black -- ModelIt bilingual platform addresses language barriers
4. **Sustained leadership**: Dr. Brawley in 13th year as superintendent -- stability enables program adoption
5. **Math gains momentum**: 6.42% math improvement in 2025 -- district investing in data-driven instruction
6. **Major industry partnerships**: Digital Promise, Apple, Verizon, Boeing, Microsoft, Google
7. **High funding**: $19,899 per-pupil spending above state median -- budget capacity exists
8. **Ranked #1 in CA test score gains**: District momentum creates openness to new tools
