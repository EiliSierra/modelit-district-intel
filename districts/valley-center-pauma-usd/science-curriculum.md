## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

ModelIt should be positioned as a **computational modeling supplement** that connects the district's existing STEAM and agricultural programs to mathematical reasoning:

1. **Agriculture/FFA connection**: Students model crop yields, water cycles, livestock populations, soil health — connecting agriculture practice to computational science. This is THE flagship connection.
2. **ForeFront Power extension**: Students already learn about solar energy through the monitoring platform. ModelIt lets them BUILD models of energy systems, not just observe data.
3. **Tribal/environmental connection**: Students model local San Diego County ecosystems, Luiseño homeland ecology, water management — culturally responsive computational modeling.
4. **Dual Immersion alignment**: ModelIt's bilingual EN/ES interface serves the 24.2% EL population and complements the award-winning Dual Immersion program.
5. **Math proficiency bridge**: 18% math proficiency is the crisis. ModelIt embeds math (variables, equations, graphs) in authentic modeling contexts that give students a reason to USE math.
6. **Robotics pathway**: As Hailwood expands robotics CTE, ModelIt provides the simulation/modeling foundation that supports engineering design.
