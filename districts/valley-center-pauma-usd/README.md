## Why Valley Center-Pauma USD

1. **Math proficiency crisis**: 18% math proficiency — 16-point gap below state average, modeling can bridge applied math
2. **New superintendent with math priority**: Hailwood explicitly named "enhancing math instruction" as a top priority
3. **Strong agriculture & FFA program**: Outstanding agriculture program with State FFA Degree recipients — natural modeling fit
4. **Tribal partnerships**: 8.3% Native American enrollment, MOU with Rincon Band — culturally responsive modeling opportunities
5. **Dual Immersion program**: Award-winning EN/ES program aligns with ModelIt's bilingual capability
6. **ForeFront Power STEAM integration**: Solar portfolio with curriculum tie-in shows district values STEAM education
7. **Career pathway expansion**: Hailwood expanding CTE into agriculture, culinary, robotics across grade levels
8. **CSUSM Partnership for Success**: University connection for PD and credibility
