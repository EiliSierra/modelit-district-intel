## Key Relationships & Notes

- **Hailwood is the priority**: New superintendent with explicit math instruction priority = active evaluator of solutions
- **18-year institutional knowledge**: Hailwood knows every teacher, parent, and program in the district — he doesn't need to be educated about the district's problems
- **Tribal sensitivity**: 8.3% Native American enrollment with formal tribal MOUs means cultural responsiveness is essential in outreach
- **Dual Immersion is a selling point**: The award-winning EN/ES program means the district already values bilingual education — ModelIt's EN/ES interface is an asset
- **Bond failure context**: The $85M bond failed in 2024, so budget is tight and the district is cost-conscious. Position ModelIt as high-value, low-cost
- **ForeFront Power precedent**: The district already embraced STEAM curriculum through the solar partnership — they understand the value of technology-curriculum integration
- **Rural challenge**: 300 sq miles covering Valley Center and Pauma communities means in-person access requires planning, but Carlsbad is only ~45 min away
