### Revenue Breakdown

| Source | % | Amount (2021-22) | Per-Pupil |
|--------|---|--------|-----------|
| Local (property taxes, fees) | 45% | $27,688,000 | ~$7,230 |
| State (LCFF base + supplemental + categorical) | 42% | $25,859,000 | ~$6,752 |
| Federal (Title I/II/IV, IDEA, ESSER) | 14% | $8,479,000 | ~$2,214 |

**Key insight**: Local revenue (45%) is the largest source, indicating a higher local property tax base than many rural districts. State funding (42%) includes LCFF supplemental/concentration given the 63% Hispanic + 24.2% EL populations. Federal at 14% includes Title I for the 43.8% economically disadvantaged population.

**Recommended Primary Pathway**: LCFF Supplemental/Concentration + Title I co-funding for a math/science intervention pilot targeting grades 5-12. Agriculture modeling as CTE entry point.

**Pricing Strategy**:
- MS/HS pilot (~1,900 students grades 6-12): $10-15K
- Elementary add-on (grades 4-5, ~250 students): $3-5K
- District-wide: $15-25K
- Position as bilingual STEM tool serving unduplicated pupils
