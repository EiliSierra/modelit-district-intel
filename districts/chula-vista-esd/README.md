## Why This District

1. **Largest elementary district in California** — 28,845 students, ~47-50 schools = flagship case study potential
2. **68.1% Hispanic, ~35% EL** — bilingual ModelIt is a natural fit
3. **EL math at 15.99%** — significant gap even in a high-performing district
4. **$360M Measure AA bond** (Nov 2024) — explicitly funds STEAM classrooms and labs
5. **Qualcomm Innovation Station** — district already values hands-on STEM
6. **Clever SSO + 23 DLI schools** — frictionless integration + bilingual program alignment
7. **Feeds into Sweetwater UHSD** (already in pipeline) — cross-district alignment story
8. **Twig Science (2023)** — ModelIt complements as computational modeling layer
