## ModelIt Positioning

### Why ModelIt Complements Twig Science
1. **Twig = content delivery**: NGSS-aligned lessons, readings, activities
2. **ModelIt = computational practice**: Students BUILD their own models (SEP 2 + SEP 5)
3. **Bilingual advantage**: ModelIt's full EN/ES interface matches CVESD's 23 DLI schools
4. **No overlap**: Twig doesn't offer computational modeling tools; ModelIt doesn't replace curriculum

### Entry Point
Twig Science was adopted in 2023 — too soon for replacement. But **supplemental tools can be adopted at any time**. ModelIt enters as a supplemental computational modeling platform, not a curriculum replacement.

### DLI Connection (23 Schools)
CVESD operates one of the fastest-growing dual language immersion programs in CA:
- 90/10 and 50/50 models across 23 schools
- ModelIt's Spanish-native interface is uniquely positioned for DLI science instruction
- Benchmark Adelante (Spanish ELA) is already in use — ModelIt adds bilingual STEM
