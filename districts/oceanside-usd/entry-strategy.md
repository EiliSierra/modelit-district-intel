## The Hook

"Oceanside USD is the only district in North County offering a biomanufacturing AS degree through MiraCosta College -- yet only 31.78% of your students meet math standards, and Hispanic students are at just 23.98%. ModelIt is an NSF-funded, bilingual computational modeling platform that lets your biotech and biomanufacturing students BUILD the systems models that connect lab work to conceptual understanding -- directly aligned to NGSS Practice 2."

## Decision Process

1. Executive Director C&I (Lawrence) identifies need
2. Directors (Smedley/Bell) evaluate for grade bands
3. Pilot approved at 2-3 schools
4. Teacher PD and implementation
5. Results reviewed by C&I team
6. Budget through LCAP or Title funding
7. Board consent calendar for purchase

## 5-Phase Approach

### Phase 1: Direct Outreach (Weeks 1-3)
- Email Richard Lawrence at richard.lawrence@oside.us with district-specific hook
- CC Greg Smedley at greg.smedley@oside.us for secondary angle
- Being 10 minutes from Carlsbad, offer immediate in-person demo
- Reference biomanufacturing/MiraCosta partnership

### Phase 2: Demo & Evaluation (Weeks 4-8)
- In-person demo at OUSD district office (2111 Mission Ave)
- Include Mandy Bell for elementary perspective
- Provide teacher trial accounts
- Connect ModelIt to biotech/biomanufacturing CTE pathways

### Phase 3: Pilot Proposal (Weeks 9-12)
- Propose pilot at 2-3 schools (1 elem, 1 middle, 1 CTE-focused)
- Budget mapped to Title IV-A or LCFF Supplemental
- Include MiraCosta dual enrollment alignment
- PD plan for participating teachers

### Phase 4: Pilot Execution (Weeks 13-26)
- Launch pilot with teacher training
- Regular check-ins with C&I team
- Collect student data
- Engage Camp Pendleton school if applicable

### Phase 5: District Adoption (Weeks 27-52)
- Present results to Lawrence and Vitale
- Scale to 23 schools
- Map to LCAP goals
- Board consent calendar approval

## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Below-median budget | HIGH | HIGH | Emphasize ROI; use multiple funding sources; start small |
| Science gap not severe enough | MEDIUM | MEDIUM | Lead with equity gap (25-point Hispanic-White math gap) |
| CTE programs self-sufficient | MEDIUM | MEDIUM | Show how modeling enhances (not duplicates) CTE work |
| Long-tenured superintendent set in ways | LOW | MEDIUM | Dr. Vitale is equity-focused -- ModelIt's bilingual platform aligns |
| MiraCosta may prefer different tools | LOW | LOW | Present ModelIt as complementary to college curriculum |
| Camp Pendleton schools have different processes | MEDIUM | LOW | Target non-military schools first; add Pendleton later |
| Teacher resistance to new platform | MEDIUM | MEDIUM | Comprehensive PD included; integrate into existing courses |
