## Why Oceanside

1. **Math gap**: 31.78% proficiency vs 34% state average -- Hispanic students at just 23.98%
2. **Local to Carlsbad**: Adjacent city, 10-minute drive for in-person demos
3. **Verified contact emails**: District publishes staff emails on website
4. **Strong CTE**: Biotech, cybersecurity, health sciences -- 12 CTE courses
5. **Military connection**: 3 schools on Camp Pendleton -- federal funding access
6. **Dual enrollment**: Highest enrollment in North County at MiraCosta College
7. **Equity-focused superintendent**: Dr. Vitale recognized for equity work by SDCOE
