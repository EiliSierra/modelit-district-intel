## Problems They're Solving

1. Below-median per-pupil spending -- need to maximize every dollar
2. Hispanic math equity gap (23.98% vs 49.42% White)
3. Science proficiency below state average (~26% vs 29%)
4. Grade 11 math at 24.86% -- college readiness concern
5. Maintaining graduation rate (88%) while improving academic achievement
6. Serving diverse population including military families at Camp Pendleton
