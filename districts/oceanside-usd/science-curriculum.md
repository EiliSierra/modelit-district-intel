## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

Oceanside has strong CTE pathways (especially the unique biomanufacturing program) but faces a moderate science gap (26% vs 29% state avg) and a significant equity gap (Hispanic math at 23.98%). ModelIt should be positioned as a tool that bridges the gap between CTE hands-on work and NGSS conceptual understanding.

**Key message**: "Your biomanufacturing and biotechnology students model biological systems in the lab. ModelIt lets them build computational models BEFORE and AFTER lab work -- deepening understanding and directly aligning to NGSS Practice 2. And with 60% Hispanic enrollment, our bilingual platform ensures every student can access the science."

### How ModelIt Complements Current Tools

| Current Tool/Program | What It Does | What ModelIt Adds |
|---------------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Biomanufacturing (MiraCosta) | Lab-based production skills | Computational modeling of biological systems |
| Biotechnology CTE | Lab techniques, biotech careers | Systems modeling of biotech processes |
| Cybersecurity CTE | Network security | Systems modeling of network architectures |
| Health Sciences CTE | Healthcare career skills | Modeling of physiological systems |
