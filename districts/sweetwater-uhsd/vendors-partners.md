## Vendor Landscape Analysis

### Where ModelIt Fits
- **Gap**: Dedicated STEAM department but likely no computational modeling platform
- **Opportunity**: $646M revenue = budget exists; 34K students = large deal potential
- **Challenge**: Below-median per-pupil spending = scrutinized purchases
- **Unique angle**: SDSU Compact = computational modeling prepares students for college STEM

### Warm Introduction Paths
1. **SDSU** -- if ModelIt/Cell Collective has SDSU connections, leverage Compact for Success
2. **STEAM Department** -- dedicated department means dedicated staff evaluating tools
3. **San Diego County network** -- other SD County districts using ModelIt create peer pressure
4. **NSF SBIR** -- research-backed platform for large district with accountability expectations

### Competitive Threats
- **PLTW** -- if present, major CTE competitor with deep institutional presence
- **District size** -- 29 schools means complex procurement and pilot logistics
- **Budget pressure** -- $15,328 per-pupil is well below state median
- **Multiple priorities** -- IB, AVID, CTE, SDSU Compact = many competing programs
