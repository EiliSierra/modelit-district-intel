## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

Sweetwater is a massive high school district (34,445 students, 29 schools) with a dedicated STEAM department and CTE pathways. The district has the infrastructure for STEM innovation -- what it needs is a computational modeling tool that connects classroom science to the STEM careers in the STEAM department's vision.

**Key message**: "Your dedicated STEAM department and CTE pathways show Sweetwater's commitment to STEM career readiness. ModelIt adds the computational modeling dimension -- letting students BUILD systems models that connect science concepts to real-world engineering and biotech careers. With 70% Hispanic enrollment and the SDSU Compact for Success, bilingual modeling skills prepare students for both college and career."
