## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

ModelIt should be positioned as a **computational modeling supplement** that extends the district's existing hands-on STEM approach into the digital/computational domain:

1. **STEM Lab connection**: The Bud Biggs STEM Lab provides the physical space for computational modeling. ModelIt is the software that makes the lab even more powerful.
2. **Future City connection**: Students already design future cities — ModelIt lets them model the SYSTEMS within those cities (energy, water, population, ecology).
3. **FFA/Agriculture connection**: Students can model crop yields, water usage, livestock populations, soil health, and desert ecology — connecting CTE to computational science.
4. **Product Design connection**: CTE Product Design & Engineering pathway already teaches iterative design — ModelIt adds computational simulation to the design process.
5. **Desert ecology**: Mojave Desert location provides unique real-world phenomena for modeling (water cycles, drought, heat, solar energy, desert ecosystems).

### How ModelIt Complements Current Tools

| Current Tool/Program | What It Does | What ModelIt Adds |
|---------------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Bud Biggs STEM Lab | Hands-on STEM exploration | Computational modeling software for the lab |

| Product Design & Engineering | Physical product design | Computational modeling in the design process |
| Destination Imagination | Creative problem-solving | Model-based investigation of complex problems |
