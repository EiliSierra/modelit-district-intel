### Revenue Breakdown

| Source | % | Amount | Per-Pupil |

**Key insight**: Per-pupil spending of $21,607 is notably high for a small district, driven by fixed costs spread across a small enrollment base. Revenue is 66% state-funded, reflecting a low local tax base.
