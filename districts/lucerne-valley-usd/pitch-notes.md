## Key Talking Points

1. **"10.69% math proficiency demands a different approach"**: "Nearly 90% of your students aren't meeting math standards. Traditional interventions aren't working. ModelIt gives students a reason to USE math — they build computational models of systems they care about. It's applied mathematics, not worksheets."

2. **"Connect your STEM investments"**: "You've invested in a STEM Lab, FFA, Destination Imagination, Future City, and CTE with 80% participation. ModelIt is the software that connects all of these to computational thinking and mathematical reasoning."

3. **"Desert as laboratory"**: "Your students live in the Mojave Desert — one of the most dynamic ecosystems in California. ModelIt turns the desert into a data source. Students model water cycles, drought patterns, solar energy systems, and desert ecology. It's math that matters because it's about their world."

4. **"FFA + ModelIt"**: "Your award-winning FFA students already work with agricultural systems. ModelIt lets them BUILD computational models of those systems — crop yields, soil health, water management, livestock populations. It's the bridge between agricultural practice and agricultural science."

5. **"CSI-eligible intervention"**: "With red Dashboard indicators, you qualify for Comprehensive Support & Improvement funding that MUST be spent on evidence-based interventions. ModelIt is NSF-funded and research-backed — it qualifies."

6. **"Product Design pathway integration"**: "Your CTE Product Design & Engineering pathway teaches iterative design. ModelIt adds computational simulation — students model before they build. It's industry-standard practice."

7. **"Bilingual by design"**: "With 57% Hispanic students and 13% English Learners, language shouldn't block access to computational thinking. ModelIt is fully bilingual — every student can build models in English or Spanish."
