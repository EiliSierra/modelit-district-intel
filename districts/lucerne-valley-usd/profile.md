### ModelIt Opportunity Analysis

- **Math**: 89.3% of students NOT meeting math standard — among the worst in the pipeline
- **Level 1 crisis**: 66.77% of students at Level 1 in math (Standard Not Met)
- **"Nearly Met" band**: 22.53% in math are close — computational modeling can push these students over
- **Dashboard "red"**: District created a new Director of Academic Programs position specifically to address red indicators
- **ELA-Math gap**: Students perform significantly better in ELA (21%) than math (11%) — math is the acute need

## Problems They're Solving

1. **Critical math/ELA proficiency crisis**: Red on CA Dashboard — 10.69% math, 20.97% ELA
2. **New leadership position**: Director of Academic Programs created specifically to improve scores
3. **CTE expansion**: Growing from 80% to 100% student participation
4. **Rural access gap**: Students in remote desert community lack enrichment opportunities
5. **EL support**: ~13% English Learners need language-accessible instruction
