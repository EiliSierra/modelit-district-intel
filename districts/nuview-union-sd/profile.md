### ModelIt Opportunity Analysis

- **Math**: 78% of students NOT meeting math standard — significant improvement opportunity
- **Science**: 82% not meeting science standard — worst subject, NGSS modeling gap is clear
- **ELA-Math gap**: Students perform better in ELA (38%) than math (22%) — math is the acute need
- **K-8 focus**: ModelIt's sweet spot (grades 5-8) IS this district's grade span
- **EL opportunity**: 23.7% English Learners + bilingual ModelIt = strong equity argument
- **Old curriculum**: Pre-NGSS science materials (Harcourt) still adopted — ripe for supplemental intervention
