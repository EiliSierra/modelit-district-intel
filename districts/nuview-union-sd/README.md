## Why NuView Union SD

1. **High-need, high-Hispanic district**: 85.3% Hispanic, 61.3% economically disadvantaged — ModelIt's bilingual EN/ES is a differentiator
2. **STEM Lab at Mountain Shadows**: Purpose-built STEM Lab serves 120+ students/day with hands-on modules — ModelIt adds computational layer
3. **Math gap**: 22% math proficiency — 12-point gap below state average; math intervention needed
4. **Science gap**: 18% science proficiency — NGSS modeling practice gap
5. **Old science curriculum**: Harcourt science (pre-NGSS) still in use — adoption cycle likely imminent
6. **Solar/energy partnership**: ENGIE solar savings funded STEM Lab — shows district invests energy savings into STEM
7. **English Learner population**: 23.7% EL — bilingual ModelIt serves this population directly
8. **K-8 sweet spot**: Grades 5-8 are ModelIt's strongest fit; this district IS grades K-8
