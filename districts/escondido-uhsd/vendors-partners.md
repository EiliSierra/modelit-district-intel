### Where ModelIt Fits
- **Gap**: District has dedicated science and math content specialists but may lack computational modeling tools
- **Opportunity**: Small district (5 schools) means faster vendor evaluation and adoption
- **Strength**: Engineering & Design CTE pathway is a natural fit for systems modeling

### Warm Introduction Paths
1. **San Diego County Office of Education** -- SDCOE often coordinates STEM initiatives across local districts
2. **NSF SBIR credibility** -- research-backed platform appeals to data-driven educators
3. **Local proximity** -- Escondido is 20 minutes from Carlsbad, enabling in-person relationship building

### Competitive Threats
- **Existing science curriculum** -- if recently adopted, may resist adding supplements
- **Free alternatives** -- PhET and similar free tools may reduce urgency
- **Budget size** -- $151M total revenue is modest; budget scrutiny may be higher
