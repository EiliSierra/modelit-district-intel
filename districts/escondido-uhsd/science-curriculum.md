## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

EUHSD is a grades 7-12 district -- the sweet spot for ModelIt. With only 20.68% math proficiency and ~20% science proficiency, there is an acute need for tools that make abstract STEM concepts accessible. ModelIt should be positioned as an NGSS Practice 2 tool that enhances both science courses and CTE Engineering pathways.

**Key message**: "Only 1 in 5 EUHSD students meets math standards, and Hispanic students are at 14.88%. ModelIt's visual, bilingual computational modeling platform makes abstract systems thinking tangible -- and it directly supports your Engineering & Design CTE pathway."
