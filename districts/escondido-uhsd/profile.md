## Problems They're Solving

1. Math proficiency crisis (20.68% vs 34% state avg -- 13-point gap)
2. Hispanic math equity gap (14.88% vs 41.95% White = 27-point gap)
3. Nearly 58% of students at minimal math level
4. Science proficiency below state average (~20% vs 29%)
5. Graduation rate at 85% (below state avg of 87%)
6. Building out CTE pathways (Engineering & Design, Del Lago Academy)
7. Transitioning under relatively new superintendent (Petersen, Jul 2023)
