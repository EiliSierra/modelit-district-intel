## Estimated Funding Profile

### LCFF Supplemental & Concentration
- **Unduplicated Pupil %**: Estimated 80-85%+ (60.6% Econ Disadvantaged + 19% EL + Foster Youth)
- **Concentration threshold**: >55% triggers additional concentration grants
- MVUSD is well above 55% -- receives both supplemental AND concentration grants
- These funds must be used for increased/improved services for unduplicated pupils
- **ModelIt fit**: Bilingual STEM platform directly serves EL and low-income students

### Federal Funding
- **Title I**: District-wide (all schools receive Title I)
- **Title III**: Significant given 5,968 EL students (19%)
- **Title IV-A (SSAE)**: Available for STEM, well-rounded education, technology
- **ESSER III**: Referenced on LCAP page (likely expended or expiring)

### Bond Funding
- **Measure X**: $240M approved November 2024
- Includes technology upgrades as explicit use case
- Generates ~$160M in matching state funds
- **Total**: ~$400M for facility and tech modernization
