**Key demographic context**: Calexico sits directly on the US–Mexico border. Over 40% of high school students are reportedly born in Mexico; 50%+ of families are low-income. The district has a formal "newcomer program" for recent immigrant students (grades 5–6). Parallel bilingual curricula (Spanish + sheltered English) run district-wide. This makes Calexico one of the most linguistically and economically homogeneous districts in California — and one of the strongest equity cases for bilingual science tools.
