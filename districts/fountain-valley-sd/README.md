## Why Fountain Valley SD

1. **Top-tier OC district**: 74% math / 79% ELA — this is a district that values academic excellence and innovation
2. **NGSS and STEM commitment**: Superintendent specifically cites NGSS and STEM initiatives; robotics at every school
3. **8 Gold Ribbon Schools**: Culture of distinction — they adopt best-in-class tools
4. **Subgroup gaps despite high overall scores**: Hispanic math at 57%, EL math at 43% — room for equity intervention
5. **Ed tech leadership**: Superintendent's doctorate is in educational technology; 1:1 Chromebooks district-wide
6. **High revenue ($112M)**: Well-funded district with capacity to invest in quality supplemental tools
7. **Diverse student body**: 37.5% Asian, 32% White, 19% Hispanic — multilingual population benefits from bilingual tools
