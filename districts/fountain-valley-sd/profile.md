### ModelIt Opportunity Analysis

- **Equity angle**: Despite high overall scores, Hispanic students at 57% math and EL at 43% math — significant internal gaps
- **Math decline by grade**: Proficiency drops from 91% (G3) to 68% (G6-8) — upper elementary/middle school intervention window
- **Enrichment positioning**: High-performing district = position ModelIt as enrichment and deeper learning, not remediation
- **NGSS alignment**: District committed to NGSS implementation — modeling is core SEP #2
- **Asian student excellence**: 86% math proficiency suggests strong STEM culture — ModelIt can extend this
