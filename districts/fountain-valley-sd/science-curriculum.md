### K-5 Science Details (Discovery Education)

- **Program**: Discovery Education *California Science*
- **NGSS alignment**: Full NGSS alignment — three-dimensional science instruction
- **Delivery**: Digital-first with hands-on investigation kits
- **Strengths**: Multimedia-rich, real-world phenomena, digital platform
- **ModelIt fit**: Discovery Education covers content delivery; ModelIt adds model-BUILDING which Discovery Ed doesn't offer. Complementary, not competing.

### 6-8 Science Details (Amplify)

- **Program**: Amplify *CA Science Program*
- **NGSS alignment**: Full NGSS alignment — phenomena-driven, three-dimensional
- **Delivery**: Digital curriculum with hands-on investigations + simulations
- **Strengths**: Student-centered, inquiry-based, strong teacher support
- **ModelIt fit**: Amplify uses pre-built simulations; ModelIt lets students BUILD models. Amplify covers Practices 1, 3, 4, 6-8; ModelIt deepens Practice 2 (modeling) and Practice 5 (math/computational thinking). Complementary.

## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

ModelIt should be positioned as a **NGSS Practice 2 enrichment tool** that extends Fountain Valley's already-strong science and math programs into computational modeling:

1. **Amplify complement (6-8)**: Amplify delivers phenomena-driven content with pre-built simulations. ModelIt lets students BUILD their own computational models — fundamentally different cognitive work. Position as "the next step in three-dimensional science."

2. **Discovery Education complement (K-5)**: Discovery Ed provides rich multimedia content. ModelIt adds the model-building dimension that Discovery Ed lacks. Start with grade 5-6 where students are ready for computational thinking.

3. **Math Expressions complement (K-5)**: HMH Math Expressions teaches conceptual math. ModelIt provides applied math through system modeling — students see mathematical relationships in real-world contexts.

4. **Middle school math gap closer**: Math proficiency drops from 85% (G3) to 68% (G6-8). ModelIt bridges this by making math meaningful through modeling — especially for the transition from elementary to middle school.

5. **Equity tool**: Hispanic students at 57% math and EL at 43% math need enrichment that's accessible. ModelIt's bilingual interface and visual modeling reduce language barriers while maintaining cognitive rigor.

6. **NGSS Practice 2 depth**: Fountain Valley is committed to NGSS. ModelIt IS NGSS Practice 2 (Developing and Using Models) — the district can deepen what's already working.
