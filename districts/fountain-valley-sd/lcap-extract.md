## Budget Entry Points for ModelIt

| Funding Source | Est. Available | Rationale | Likelihood |
|---------------|---------------|-----------|-----------|
| **LCFF Supplemental/Concentration** | $50-100K | Equity gaps: Hispanic math 57%, EL math 43%. S&C funds target these populations. | HIGH |
| **General Fund (Instructional Materials)** | $30-50K | Enrichment tool for a well-funded district that values excellence | HIGH |
| **Title I** | $20-40K | Pilot at Title I schools (Cox, Masuda, Oka, Plavan) | MEDIUM |
| **Measure O Bond** | $10-30K | Technology for math/science instruction (bond language) | MEDIUM |
| **Site Discretionary** | $5-15K per school | Individual schools can purchase supplemental tools | MEDIUM |
| **GATE/Enrichment Funds** | $10-20K | GATE program alignment — extend high performers | LOW-MEDIUM |

**Recommended Primary Pathway**: LCFF Supplemental/Concentration for equity + General Fund for enrichment. Position ModelIt as both: closes gaps for Hispanic/EL students AND deepens learning for Asian/White high performers.

**Pricing Strategy**:
- Large suburban district with 6,022 students — premium pricing justified
- Pilot at 2-3 middle schools: $10-15K
- Elementary expansion: $15-25K
- District-wide: $30-50K
- Position as NGSS Practice 2 enrichment with equity benefits — NOT remediation
