## Key Talking Points

1. **"NGSS Practice 2 — the missing piece"**: "Fountain Valley has strong science content through Amplify and Discovery Ed. But NGSS Practice 2 — Developing and Using Models — requires students to BUILD computational models, not just interact with them. That's exactly what ModelIt does. It's the depth layer that completes three-dimensional science."

2. **"From 85% to 68% — the middle school math cliff"**: "Your 3rd graders are at 85% math proficiency — extraordinary. By 6th grade, it's 68%. That 17-point drop happens during the transition from concrete to abstract math. ModelIt bridges that gap by making abstract math visible — students SEE mathematical relationships in their models."

3. **"Enrichment that closes equity gaps"**: "74% math proficiency is exceptional. But Hispanic students at 57% and ELs at 43% means the rising tide hasn't lifted all boats equally. ModelIt is fully bilingual (English/Spanish) and uses visual modeling that reduces language barriers while maintaining high cognitive demand."

4. **"Computational thinking for an innovation community"**: "Fountain Valley parents include engineers, tech professionals, and entrepreneurs (Board Member Ramirez leads UCI Innovation & Entrepreneurship). These families expect cutting-edge STEM education. ModelIt delivers the computational thinking that prepares students for careers in science, engineering, and data science."

5. **"Complements, never competes"**: "We're not asking you to replace Amplify, Discovery Ed, or your math curriculum. ModelIt is a supplemental enrichment tool that makes your existing investments more powerful. Students use Amplify to explore phenomena, then use ModelIt to BUILD the computational models that explain them."

6. **"NSF-backed, not a startup experiment"**: "ModelIt is funded by NSF's rigorous SBIR Phase II program — that means it's been reviewed by national science education experts and deemed worthy of federal investment. When you adopt ModelIt, you're backed by the same agency that funds university research."

7. **"ENGIE Living Labs + ModelIt"**: "Your ENGIE partnership gives students hands-on experience with energy and weather systems. ModelIt lets them BUILD computational models of those systems — predict energy production, model weather patterns, simulate climate scenarios. It's the digital lab that extends your physical labs."

8. **"GATE extension"**: "Your gifted students are ready for advanced challenges. ModelIt scales from introductory models to university-level complexity. GATE students can build multi-variable systems models that would challenge AP students. It's open-ended enrichment with no ceiling."

## Objection Handling

| # | Objection | Response |
|---|-----------|----------|
| 1 | "We're already at 74% math — we don't need intervention tools." | "Absolutely — and that's not what ModelIt is. It's enrichment, not intervention. Your top performers get deeper NGSS modeling. Your middle school students get the applied math context that prevents the 85%-to-68% drop. And your Hispanic/EL students get bilingual access to computational thinking. It lifts every group." |
| 2 | "We already have Amplify for science." | "Amplify is excellent for phenomena-driven content and pre-built simulations. ModelIt adds a fundamentally different capability: students BUILD their own models. Amplify is content delivery; ModelIt is cognitive tool. They're complementary — together they complete three-dimensional science." |
| 3 | "We use Discovery Education for K-5." | "Discovery Ed is a rich content platform. ModelIt adds the construction layer — upper elementary students (G5-6) move from watching and reading about science to building computational models. It's the difference between learning ABOUT models and BUILDING models." |
| 4 | "Our teachers are already managing many platforms." | "ModelIt integrates with your existing infrastructure — it runs on Chromebooks through Clever SSO. No new devices, no new logins. We provide turnkey PD so teachers can integrate ModelIt into their existing Amplify/Discovery Ed lessons, not create new lesson plans from scratch." |
| 5 | "Budget is tight with Measure O projects." | "Measure O is facilities — separate budget category. ModelIt is instructional, funded through General Fund or LCFF Supplemental/Concentration. Your S&C allocation of ~$3.2M specifically targets the equity gaps ModelIt addresses. A pilot is $10-15K — less than 1% of S&C." |
| 6 | "We'd want to see evidence of effectiveness." | "ModelIt is NSF SBIR Phase II-funded — one of the most rigorous federal review processes for educational tools. We'd love to run a pilot at Masuda Middle (Title I) and collect formal effectiveness data. Let's build the evidence base together." |
| 7 | "Our IXL and Khan Academy already cover math supplemental." | "IXL and Khan focus on skill practice — solving individual problems. ModelIt focuses on systems thinking — building models where multiple mathematical relationships interact. Different cognitive work, different outcomes. ModelIt develops the mathematical reasoning that transfers to CAASPP, not just the procedural skills." |
