## Purchasing & Warrants

> Board meeting consent calendars typically include purchasing orders (POs) and warrants (payment records). These reveal who the district is paying, for how much, and for what. Key items to extract:

### Items to Track from Board Agendas

| Item Type | What to Look For | Why It Matters |
|-----------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Warrants (Check Register)** | Monthly payment registers listing vendor names and amounts | Reveals current vendors, contract sizes, spending patterns |
| **Purchase Orders** | New POs for instructional materials, technology, services | Shows what's being bought and from whom |
| **Contracts** | Service agreements, curriculum licenses, PD contracts | Identifies competitors and pricing benchmarks |
| **Bids/RFPs** | Requests for proposals or bid results | Signals upcoming purchasing opportunities |
| **Budget Transfers** | Movement of funds between categories | Shows where money is being redirected |

### Extraction Template for Warrant/PO Review

When reviewing board consent calendars, flag:

- [ ] Any payments to curriculum publishers (Amplify, Discovery Ed, McGraw Hill, HMH, Benchmark)
- [ ] EdTech platform payments (IXL, Khan Academy, Desmos, BrainPOP, etc.)
- [ ] Technology hardware purchases (Chromebooks, iPads, networking)
- [ ] Professional development vendor payments
- [ ] STEM equipment/material purchases
- [ ] Science lab supply orders
- [ ] Assessment platform costs (NWEA MAP, iReady, etc.)
