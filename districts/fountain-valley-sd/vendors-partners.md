## Vendor Landscape Analysis

### Where ModelIt Fits

| Position | Assessment |
|----------|-----------|
| **Direct competitors present** | NONE — no computational modeling tools identified |
| **Complementary tools** | Amplify (science) + Discovery Ed (science) + IXL/Desmos (math) → ModelIt adds model-BUILDING |
| **Gap ModelIt fills** | NGSS Practice 2 (Developing and Using Models) — no current tool addresses this |
| **Price sensitivity** | LOW — affluent district, $112M revenue, premium tool investments |

### Warm Intro Paths

1. **Board Member Dennis Cole**: Chief of Staff at Orange County Department of Education (OCDE). Education insider who can validate ModelIt's NSF credentials and NGSS alignment. Warm path through OCDE network.

2. **Board Member Ashley Ramirez**: Leads recruitment at UCI Innovation & Entrepreneurship. Innovation-oriented perspective; could connect to UCI research partnerships.

3. **Board Member Steve Schultz**: Current Fountain Valley High teacher, 2x Teacher of the Year. Classroom practitioner who understands instructional tools from a teacher's perspective.

4. **Superintendent Dr. Katherine Stopp**: Pepperdine MA in Educational Technology. Technology-forward leader who understands and values edtech tools. Direct approach feasible.

5. **ENGIE Living Labs connection**: If ModelIt can create energy/weather/sustainability models that complement ENGIE Living Labs, this creates a natural integration story.

6. **Fountain Valley Educational Foundation**: Community foundation could co-fund a pilot or host a presentation.

### Competitive Threats

| Threat | Level | Rationale |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Amplify expanding into modeling | MEDIUM | Amplify has simulations but not student model-building. Could add features. |
| Discovery Ed adding modeling tools | LOW | DE is content-focused; unlikely to build modeling platform |
| Free tools (PhET, NetLogo) | LOW | Pre-built simulations only; no student model creation |
| Status quo ("we're doing fine") | MEDIUM-HIGH | 74% math proficiency reduces urgency. Lead with equity gaps and NGSS depth. |
| Budget competing priorities | MEDIUM | Measure O bond may absorb attention. Position ModelIt as instructional, not facilities. |
