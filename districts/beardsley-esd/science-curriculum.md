## What This Means for ModelIt

### Current State
Beardsley is operating with:
1. **No confirmed core science publisher** — students may receive inconsistent science instruction across 4 schools
2. **PLTW electives only** at JH level — PLTW Gateway is 3-5% of instructional time, not a full science program
3. **13.29% math, 11.37% science** — both well below state averages; students are not building conceptual models in science OR math
4. **EL students at 3.28% science** — the EL population is nearly completely excluded from science proficiency
