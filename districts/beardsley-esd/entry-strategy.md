## The Hook

> "Beardsley Junior High already uses PLTW to teach students how to design systems — but your CAST science score of **11.37%** (Grade 8) shows that students aren't yet connecting design thinking to scientific modeling. ModelIt is the missing link: it lets students BUILD computational science models, not just manipulate pre-built PLTW simulations. With Mac Robertson already running Design & Modeling AND RTI Math, we can launch a 6-week pilot in Spring 2026 that generates measurable CAST-ready outcomes — at no cost to your PLTW budget."

**Why this hook works**:
1. References SPECIFIC district data (11.37% CAST, BJHS, Mac Robertson by name)
2. Complements PLTW — does not compete
3. Offers a concrete timeline (6-week pilot)
4. Addresses the budget concern immediately ("no cost to PLTW budget")
5. Bilingual/EL angle available as secondary hook: "3.28% EL science scores — your English Learners are nearly locked out of science. ModelIt is bilingual English/Spanish."
