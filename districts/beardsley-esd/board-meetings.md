## Purchasing & Warrants Section

*Board consent calendars typically include purchase orders, vendor contracts, warrant registers, and budget transfers. This section tracks spending patterns relevant to ModelIt competitive intelligence.*

### Extraction Template for Board Minutes PDF Review

When reviewing board minutes PDFs, flag the following items:

**Curriculum & Instructional Materials**:
- [ ] Science textbook/program purchases (any publisher)
- [ ] Math curriculum purchases (i-Ready, Eureka, enVision, etc.)
- [ ] ELA curriculum purchases
- [ ] PLTW annual license or contract renewal
- [ ] Supplemental curriculum or program purchases

**EdTech Platforms**:
- [ ] LMS contracts (Canvas, Google, Schoology, etc.)
- [ ] Assessment platform contracts (iReady, NWEA, FastBridge, etc.)
- [ ] Student device purchases (Chromebooks, iPads, etc.)
- [ ] Clever, PowerSchool contract renewals
- [ ] Any new EdTech platform approvals

**Professional Development**:
- [ ] PD vendor payments (PLTW, curriculum publishers, etc.)
- [ ] NGSS training or science PD contracts

**STEM Equipment**:
- [ ] Robotics equipment (consistent with PLTW Automation & Robotics)
- [ ] Lab materials or science equipment
- [ ] 3D printers or design tools (consistent with PLTW Design & Modeling)
