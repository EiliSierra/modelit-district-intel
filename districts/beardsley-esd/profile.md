**CRITICAL NOTE on SED/EL Discrepancy**:
- The CDE baseline provided states SED = 16.5% and EL = 1.6%. However, the CDE district profile page itself shows 249 EL students (12.8% of 1,946 enrolled). The SED figure of 16.5% is implausible given the district's CEP participation — CEP requires ≥40% of students to be identified as needy through USDA means. The actual FRPM-eligible percentage is almost certainly well above 40%.
- **Working assumption**: EL = 12.8% (CDE profile 2026); SED/FRPM = 40%+ (implied by CEP). Recommend verifying unduplicated pupil count from 2025-26 LCAP document.
- **For LCAP calculation**: LCFF Supplemental funds are significant ($9.3M projection for 2025-26) given the unduplicated count of foster youth, EL, and low-income students.
