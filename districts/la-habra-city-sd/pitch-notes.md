## Key Talking Points

1. **"11.78% EL math proficiency demands bilingual tools"**: "Only 12% of your English Learners are meeting math standards. That's not a math problem — it's a language access problem. ModelIt is fully bilingual. Your EL students can build computational models in Spanish, see the math working visually, and learn scientific reasoning without the English barrier."

2. **"Your STEAM Academies deserve computational modeling"**: "Walnut Elementary and Imperial MS have Science & Technology Academy identities. But STEAM without computational modeling is incomplete. ModelIt is the 'M' in STEAM made real — students model mathematical relationships in scientific systems."

3. **"PLTW + ModelIt = complete STEM"**: "PLTW Gateway teaches design, robotics, and coding. ModelIt adds systems modeling — students don't just build things, they simulate how systems behave. It's the bridge between engineering and science."

4. **"$73M bond + new science labs = new software opportunity"**: "Measure O is upgrading your science labs starting Summer 2026. New labs need new tools. ModelIt is the computational platform that makes your investment produce 21st-century thinkers."

5. **"Math proficiency drops from Grade 3 to Grade 7"**: "Your students go from 49% math proficiency in 3rd grade to 30% by 7th grade. The math gets abstract, and students lose the thread. Computational modeling makes abstract math concrete — students see variables, rates, and relationships working in real systems."

6. **"NSF-funded, not a startup experiment"**: "ModelIt is backed by NSF SBIR Phase II funding. The federal government's most rigorous scientific review process validated our approach. This isn't untested — it's research-backed."

7. **"Runs on what you already have"**: "You've already invested in 1:1 Chromebooks and iPads. ModelIt is web-based — no new hardware, no installations, no IT headaches. It works on every device in your district today."

## Objection Handling

| # | Objection | Response |
|---|-----------|----------|
| 1 | "We already have PLTW for STEM." | "Great — PLTW teaches design and engineering. ModelIt adds computational modeling and systems thinking. They serve different NGSS practices. PLTW is Practice 6 (Design Solutions). ModelIt is Practice 2 (Developing Models) and Practice 5 (Using Math). They're better together." |
| 2 | "Our focus is math instruction methods, not new tools." | "ModelIt IS a math instruction method. Computational modeling is mathematical discourse in action — students define variables, set rates, test hypotheses with data, and analyze graphs. It's everything your LCAP describes, in a platform." |
| 3 | "We're consumed with Measure O right now." | "We want to be part of that momentum, not add to your workload. When your new science labs open, they'll need computational tools. Let us be ready when you are — a small pilot this year proves the concept before the labs open." |
| 4 | "Budget is tight — we just passed a bond." | "The bond is facilities. ModelIt comes from your operating budget — Title I (80% ED qualifies), LCFF Supplemental (86% Hispanic + 21% EL), or Title III (bilingual tool). A pilot is $10-15K at two schools." |
| 5 | "Our EL students aren't ready for computational tools." | "That's exactly backward. EL students often excel with visual, interactive tools because they reduce dependence on English text. ModelIt's bilingual interface meets students in their language. Our research shows EL students engage MORE with model-building because it's visual and hands-on." |
| 6 | "We need to see evidence it works." | "ModelIt is NSF SBIR Phase II funded — that's federal evidence validation. We can also provide research publications and pilot data from comparable districts. Let's run a pilot at your STEAM Academy sites and measure the impact." |
| 7 | "We don't have a STEM coordinator to manage this." | "Dr. Sandoval and your Academic Coach Jody Green are all you need. ModelIt includes teacher training as part of implementation. We train your teachers, provide ongoing support, and the platform runs on your existing devices." |
