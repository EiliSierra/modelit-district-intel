## LCAP Priority Areas (2024-25)

From the 2024-25 LCAP and public reporting:

1. **Mathematics instruction and student discourse** — Strong emphasis on building mathematical reasoning through discourse strategies
2. **Universal instructional program** — Resources, staffing, and PD refocused on strong Tier 1 instruction
3. **Targeted support for subgroups** — English Learners, economically disadvantaged, students with disabilities
4. **School safety and climate** — Social-emotional wellness, behavior support
