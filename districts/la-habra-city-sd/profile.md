**Key Demographic Notes**:
- Predominantly Hispanic/Latino district (86.2%) — among the highest in the pipeline
- Very high poverty rate (80.3% economically disadvantaged)

- Suburban district in northwestern Orange County, covering La Habra and parts of Brea/Fullerton
- Community is working-class with strong ties to the school system

### ModelIt Opportunity Analysis

- **Math for EL students**: Only 11.78% meeting standard — bilingual ModelIt is a direct intervention
- **Math decline by grade**: Proficiency drops from 48.57% (G3) to 30.29% (G7) — upper elementary/middle school is the intervention window
- **SED math gap**: 28.22% for economically disadvantaged — Title I/LCFF funding alignment
- **Steady improvement trajectory**: District is trending upward — ModelIt can accelerate gains
- **Grade 5-7 sweet spot**: Math proficiency collapses from G3 to G5+ — computational modeling builds conceptual math reasoning at the critical transition

## Problems They're Solving

1. **Math proficiency for English Learners**: Only 11.78% meeting standard — district's LCAP emphasis on math
2. **SED achievement gap**: 28.22% math proficiency for economically disadvantaged vs. 33.47% overall
3. **Modernizing facilities**: $73M Measure O bond to upgrade science labs and classrooms
4. **Mathematics instruction improvement**: Explicitly prioritized in 2024-25 LCAP with new discourse strategies
5. **EL support and reclassification**: 20.6% EL population needs language-accessible tools

## Superintendent Profile

**Dr. Mario A. Carlos, Ed.D.** - Superintendent
- **Tenure**: Since July 2022 (~4 years)
- **Background**: 20+ years in La Habra district — started as 5th grade teacher at Walnut Elementary (2000)
- **Education**: B.A. Sociology/Chicano Studies (UC Berkeley), Teaching Credential (CSUSB), M.A. Counseling (Azusa Pacific), M.A. Ed Administration (UCLA), Ed.D. (Cal State Fullerton)
- **Career Path**: Teacher → Staff Specialist → Asst. Principal → Principal (Washington MS, 10 years, CA Gold Ribbon) → Director of Communications/Special Programs → Associate Superintendent of HR → Superintendent
- **Notable**: Washington MS earned CA Gold Ribbon and High-Achieving Title I School under his leadership; deep community roots; bilingual background (Chicano Studies, counseling)
- **Priorities**: Mathematics instruction, student discourse, universal instructional program with targeted support
