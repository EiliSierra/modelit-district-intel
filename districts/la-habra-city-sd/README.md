## Why La Habra City SD

1. **Massive equity opportunity**: 86% Hispanic, 80% economically disadvantaged, 21% English Learners — ModelIt's bilingual feature is a direct fit
2. **$73M Measure O bond**: Upgrading science labs and classrooms — new tools needed to match new facilities
3. **Science & Technology Academy**: STEAM programs at Imperial MS and Walnut Elementary — existing STEM culture
4. **Math gap for subgroups**: EL students at 11.78% math proficiency, SED at 28.22% — urgent intervention needed
5. **PLTW at middle schools**: Already using Project Lead the Way modules — computational modeling is the natural next step
6. **New Ed Services leadership**: Dr. Sandoval started January 2024 — actively shaping curriculum direction
7. **1:1 device infrastructure**: Chromebooks grades 2-8 and iPads at middle schools — technology-ready classrooms
