## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

ModelIt should be positioned as a **computational modeling supplement** that extends the district's STEAM academy and PLTW programs into deeper mathematical reasoning:

1. **Science & Technology Academy fit**: Walnut Elementary and Imperial MS already have STEAM identities. ModelIt adds the computational modeling layer that NGSS demands (SEP #2: Developing and Using Models).
2. **PLTW complement**: PLTW Gateway teaches design and engineering. ModelIt adds systems modeling and mathematical simulation — the computational thinking piece.
3. **Math intervention angle**: With 33.47% math proficiency and 56.7 points below standard, the LCAP emphasizes math. ModelIt makes math meaningful through applied modeling — students USE math to build models.
4. **Bilingual leverage**: 86.2% Hispanic and 21% EL population with 11.78% EL math proficiency. ModelIt's bilingual EN/ES interface directly serves this population.
5. **Measure O alignment**: $73M bond upgrading science labs — new labs need new software. ModelIt is the computational tool for the modernized science lab.
