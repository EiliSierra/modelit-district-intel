## ModelIt Positioning

### Why ModelIt Complements (Not Competes)
1. **STEMscopes gap**: STEMscopes covers NGSS content but does NOT provide computational modeling tools for SEP 2 (Developing and Using Models) or SEP 5 (Using Mathematics)
2. **Gizmos gap**: Gizmos offers pre-built simulations students interact with; ModelIt lets students BUILD their own models — fundamentally different pedagogical approach
3. **Bilingual advantage**: Neither STEMscopes nor Gizmos offers full bilingual EN/ES interface at ModelIt's level
4. **K-5 gap**: No confirmed simulation/modeling tool for elementary — ModelIt fills this entirely

### Recommended Pitch Frame
"STEMscopes gives your teachers the NGSS content. Gizmos gives secondary students pre-built simulations to explore. ModelIt gives ALL students — K through 12, in English or Spanish — the ability to BUILD computational models from scratch. It's the missing third pillar: content (STEMscopes) + exploration (Gizmos) + creation (ModelIt)."
