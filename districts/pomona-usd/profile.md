## STEM Landscape

### Programs
- **18 CTE pathways** across 7 high schools (Aviation, Cisco Networking, Game Design, Health Sciences, Manufacturing, Construction, etc.)
- **La Verne Science & Technology Charter School** (K-6) — dedicated STEM charter with science lab, robotics, coding
- **Palomares Academy of Health Sciences** — health careers focus
- **CTEC at Fairplex** — Automotive, green energy, urban farming, welding
- **Fremont Academy of Engineering & Design** — Game Design, Graphic Design, Manufacturing
- **Todd Family Foundation** — $50K endowments for STEM school labs

### CTE Pathways by School
- Diamond Ranch HS: Cisco, Game Design, Hospitality, Health, Construction
- Fremont Academy: Game Design, Graphic Design, Manufacturing
- Ganesha HS: Education, Small Business, Construction
- Garey HS: Aviation, Cisco, Game Design, Health, Law Enforcement, Marketing, Construction
- Park West HS: Hospitality, Health, Construction
- Pomona HS: Automotive, Cisco, Game Design, Health
- Village Academy: Broadcasting, Film, Health, Hospitality

### Partnerships
- Cal Poly Pomona (Project CAMINOS STEM Academy, STARS program)
- Mt. San Antonio College (dual enrollment CTE)
- College, Career & Workforce Development Task Force (17-year partnership, 30-50 members)
- SoCalGas, Fairplex, Western States Carpenters, Bright Prospect

## Problems They're Solving (from LCAP)

1. **Math proficiency crisis** — 23.97% overall, 7.28% EL
2. **EL achievement gap** — 25% of students are EL with massive proficiency gaps
3. **Chronic absenteeism** — improving (down 13%) but still a challenge
4. **Facility upgrades** — $385M Measure UU for labs, CTE spaces, safety
