## Hook

"7.28% of your English Learners meet math standards. ModelIt is the only NSF-funded computational modeling platform with a fully bilingual English/Spanish interface — and it integrates with the Clever SSO and Chromebooks you already have. It complements STEMscopes with the computational modeling layer that NGSS SEP 2 and SEP 5 require."

## Entry Path

### Phase 1: Ed Services Contact
1. Email Lilia Fuentes (Deputy Supt.) — EL math gap + bilingual platform angle
2. CC Dr. Rosario Ambriz (Director, C&I PreK-Elementary) for K-6 track
3. CC Monica Principe (Admin Director, Secondary) for 6-12 track

### Phase 2: Discovery
4. Request 15-min call or Zoom
5. Key questions: How is STEMscopes going? What's missing for NGSS SEP 2/5? What does your EL math intervention look like?
6. Confirm Clever integration requirements

### Phase 3: Pilot
7. Offer free 6-week pilot at 3-5 schools
8. Target schools with highest EL concentration
9. Frame budget under Title III (EL-specific) or Measure UU (new lab tools)
