## STEM Landscape

### Known Programs
- **STEM/STEAM Enhancement** — District-wide stated priority; curriculum specialists provide structured STEM support
- **GATE Program** — Gifted and Talented; GATE instructors use DonorsChoose for STEAM challenge materials
- **Edible Schoolyard** — Garden and kitchen classroom at select schools; science/nutrition/agriculture focus; partnership with Grimm Family Education Foundation
- **Camp KEEP** — Free hands-on outdoor/environmental learning for all 6th graders
- **Dual Language Immersion** — Mountain View TK-8 School; bilingual credential program aligned to State Seal of Biliteracy
- **VAPA (Visual & Performing Arts)** — District-wide program (part of STEAM)
- **DonorsChoose** — 3,807 projects funded across district; many STEM/science/math

## Leadership Profiles

### Superintendent: Dr. Katie Russell
- **Since:** August 2020
- **Historic first:** First woman and first woman of color in 150-year district history
- **Background:** Over 25 years in education; guided district through COVID-19 pandemic
- **Philosophy:** "Relationship building and growth" — emphasizes diversity as strength
- **Priority:** Equity, bilingual education, community partnerships

## District Context & News

- **150th Anniversary (2025):** P-BVUSD celebrated 150 years serving Bakersfield — strong community identity and pride
- **Diverse growth:** The district has grown from a one-room schoolhouse to 26 campuses; increasing diversity (62% Hispanic, growing Asian community)
