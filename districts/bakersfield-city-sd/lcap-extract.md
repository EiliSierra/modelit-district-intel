## Key LCAP Data Points for Sales Conversations

1. **$123M in Supplemental/Concentration funds** — generated for high-needs students; this is the largest available funding bucket
2. **Priority 4 is explicitly about science access** — board-adopted goal to increase science in all elementary classrooms; ModelIt is a direct response
3. **Priority 2 targets >1 year math growth** — computational modeling integrates math into science contexts, supporting acceleration
4. **EL math at 5.44%, EL science at 4.37%** — among the lowest EL subgroup scores in the state; bilingual tools are not optional, they are essential
5. **$810M total LCAP budget** — this is a very large district with significant purchasing power
