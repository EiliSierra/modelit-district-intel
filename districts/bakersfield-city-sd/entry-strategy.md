## Why BCSD is a High-Priority Opportunity

1. **Largest K-8 district in Kern County** — 30,760 students, 47 schools = significant case study potential
2. **LCAP Priority 4 = explicit science goal** — board-adopted mandate to increase science access in all elementary classrooms
3. **Devastating EL gaps** — 4.37% CAST science, 5.44% CAASPP math for EL students = bottom of the state
4. **New superintendent = new buying cycle** — Dr. Aguilera-Fort confirmed January 2026; bilingual education champion and CABE president
5. **82.8% Hispanic** — ModelIt's bilingual EN/ES interface is a rare differentiator in a predominantly Hispanic district
6. **Established vendor pathway** — Board recently approved $899K+ (Thinking Maps), $232K (Youth Cinema), $4.5K (STEM Fuse) = they buy EdTech
7. **Math decline from Grade 3 to Grade 8** — 22.59% to 11.18% = middle school is the highest-impact intervention point

## Unique Angles for BCSD

1. **CABE connection**: Dr. Aguilera-Fort is president of the California Association of Bilingual Education — ModelIt's bilingual platform is a CABE-aligned tool. This is the strongest superintendent hook in the entire pipeline.
2. **Grade 7-8 math crisis**: 11.18% proficiency = bottom decile statewide. Computational modeling integrates math into science, providing a different pathway to math growth (LCAP Priority 2).
3. **KUTR Partnership**: CSU Bakersfield teacher residency program is NGSS-aligned — ModelIt can be integrated into new teacher training as an NGSS SEP tool.
4. **Dual Immersion schools**: Horace Mann DI and other sites are natural first pilots for the Spanish-language interface.
