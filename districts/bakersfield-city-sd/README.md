1. **Critical science gap**: 15.58% CAST proficiency = bottom quartile in California; EL subgroup at 4.37% science — ModelIt's bilingual (English/Spanish) interface directly addresses this gap
2. **LCAP Priority 4 = explicit science goal**: The district has a board-adopted goal to "increase access to science and social studies content in all elementary classrooms" — ModelIt's computational modeling directly fulfills this mandate
3. **Largest K-8 district in Kern County**: 30,760 students across 47 schools = significant scale; 10 junior high schools are prime Grade 6–8 CAST pilots
4. **New superintendent, new buying cycle**: Dr. Aguilera-Fort confirmed permanent January 2026; bilingual education expert and CABE president — ModelIt's bilingual platform aligns to her core advocacy
5. **Math proficiency at 15%**: LCAP Priority 2 targets math growth; ModelIt's computational thinking and SEP-5 (Using Mathematics) directly supports math-science integration
6. **EL population alignment**: 4.5% EL + 82.8% Hispanic + new bilingual-focused superintendent = ModelIt's English/Spanish interface is a rare differentiator
