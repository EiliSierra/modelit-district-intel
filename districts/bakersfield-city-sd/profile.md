## Superintendent Profile

**Dr. Karling Aguilera-Fort**
- **Title**: 16th Superintendent, Bakersfield City School District
- **Permanent Appointment**: January 27, 2026 (contract through June 30, 2028)
- **Interim Since**: July 15, 2025 (following Mark Luque's surprise May 2025 resignation)
- **Previous Roles**: Superintendent, Oxnard School District; Deputy Superintendent, San Francisco Unified School District
- **Career Start**: Spanish Bilingual Special Education teacher (~30 years experience)
- **Education**: Ed.D. in Educational Leadership (East Carolina University, hosted in Thailand; International/Global Leadership); M.Ed., UC Berkeley; Special Ed Credential, San Jose State
- **CABE**: President, California Association of Bilingual Education Board of Directors (third term) — a major signal of multilingual/EL commitment
- **Known Priorities**: Bilingual/EL students, equity, MTSS, community partnerships, multilingual learner advocacy
- **Context**: Took over amid board tensions that led to previous superintendent's departure. Now confirmed permanent with board unanimity — new chapter for district leadership.
- **ModelIt relevance**: Her bilingual education background and CABE presidency make her exceptionally receptive to ModelIt's English/Spanish interface. This is a differentiated hook unique to BCSD.

**Prior Superintendent**: Mark Luque — announced surprise resignation May 27, 2025; departed end of August 2025; now Chief Training & Advancement Officer at KCSOS.
