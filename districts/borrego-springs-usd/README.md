## Why Borrego Springs

1. **Massive math gap**: 15.08% proficiency vs 35% state average - 20 point deficit, strongest need case in our pipeline
2. **87% Hispanic + bilingual ModelIt**: Perfect demographic match for bilingual platform
3. **Anza-Borrego Desert State Park**: Adjacent to one of CA's largest state parks - desert ecology modeling opportunity
4. **High per-pupil spending**: $29,546/student means funding IS available (issue is instructional quality, not money)
5. **CTE expansion priority**: Superintendent explicitly prioritizing career technical education
6. **Community Schools model**: District embracing wraparound services = receptive to new tools
7. **Remote location = underserved**: Few vendors actively serve this isolated district
