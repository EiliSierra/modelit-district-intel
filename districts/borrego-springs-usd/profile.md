### ModelIt Opportunity Analysis

- **Math crisis**: 64.25% of students NOT meeting math standard at Level 1 - this is the most severe math gap in our pipeline
- **Nearly Met band**: 20.67% in math are near proficiency - computational modeling could bridge that gap

- **Improvement trajectory**: ELA improved 6.65 pts from 2024 to 2025 - district is making progress, receptive to intervention tools

## Problems They're Solving (from LCAP/Strategic Plan)

1. **Severe academic underperformance**: 15% math proficiency, 24% ELA - massive gaps to state averages
2. **Geographic isolation**: 90 min from San Diego, limited professional development and vendor access
3. **CTE expansion**: Superintendent prioritizing career pathways to keep students engaged
4. **Equity and access**: 87% Hispanic, 57% economically disadvantaged - significant equity gaps
5. **Teacher recruitment/retention**: Remote location makes staffing challenging
6. **Digital access**: Initiatives to enhance digital learning tools and equitable device/internet access
7. **Community engagement**: Community Schools model to provide wraparound services
