## Decision Process

In a 345-student K-12 district, the process is the simplest possible:

1. **Superintendent evaluates** → Dr. Stevens reviews the tool
2. **Teacher validates** → Science/math teacher confirms classroom fit
3. **Superintendent approves purchase** → Likely has authority for purchases under $10K
4. **Board approval** → Only if exceeding superintendent's authority threshold
5. **Implementation** → District-wide or targeted grade band

**No RFP process**. No committee. No multi-month evaluation cycle. If Stevens likes it, it can happen in one meeting.
