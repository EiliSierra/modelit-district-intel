## Problems They're Solving

1. Hispanic math equity gap (24.05% vs 54.99% White = 31 points)
2. English Learner math proficiency at catastrophic 4.43%
3. Math proficiency drops from 45.7% (Grade 3) to 28.85% (Grade 6)
4. Below-median per-pupil spending -- need efficiency
5. Graduation rate declining (85%, down from 89% five years ago)
6. Building Career Superhighway vision into classroom practice
7. Maintaining innovation momentum across 27 schools
