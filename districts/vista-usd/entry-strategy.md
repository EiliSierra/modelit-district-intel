## Decision Process (Blueprint-Driven)

1. Superintendent identifies alignment with Blueprint vision
2. Educational Excellence department evaluates tool
3. Pilot at select schools (2-3)
4. Results measured against Blueprint goals
5. Budget approved through LCAP/Blueprint
6. Board consent calendar
7. Scale across 27 schools

## 5-Phase Approach

### Phase 1: Superintendent Engagement (Weeks 1-4)
- Email superintendentsoffice@vistausd.org with Career Superhighway/Blueprint hook
- Reference STEM2Career Expo and EL math proficiency crisis (4.43%)
- Offer in-person meeting at VUSD (15 min from Carlsbad)
- Call main office to identify Ed Services/Curriculum leader

### Phase 2: Demo & Blueprint Alignment (Weeks 5-10)
- Present ModelIt as Blueprint-aligned innovation tool
- Show how it fits Career Superhighway pathway
- Demo with Ed Services team
- Provide teacher trial accounts

### Phase 3: Pilot Proposal (Weeks 11-16)
- Propose pilot at 3 schools (1 elem, 1 middle, 1 high)
- Align explicitly with Blueprint goals and LCAP priorities
- Include bilingual features for EL students
- Map budget to Title IV-A or Blueprint innovation funds

### Phase 4: Pilot Execution (Weeks 17-30)
- Launch pilot with teacher PD
- Regular check-ins with Ed Services
- Collect engagement and achievement data
- Present at STEM2Career Expo (if timing aligns)

### Phase 5: District Adoption (Weeks 31-52)
- Present results to Dr. Doyle
- Blueprint integration plan for 27 schools
- Full PD rollout
- Board consent calendar
