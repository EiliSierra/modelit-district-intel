## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

Vista USD is an innovation-forward district led by a superintendent who personally champions STEM and career readiness through his Blueprint and Career Superhighway. ModelIt should be positioned as a natural fit for the Blueprint's innovation vision.

**Key message**: "Dr. Doyle's Career Superhighway envisions an uninterrupted career development pathway. ModelIt adds the computational modeling layer that connects students' science learning to STEM career skills -- and with your STEM2Career Expo already showing the district's commitment, ModelIt is the tool that makes it hands-on all year long."
