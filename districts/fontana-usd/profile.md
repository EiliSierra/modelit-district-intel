### ModelIt Opportunity Analysis

- **Math crisis across all grades**: 77% of students NOT meeting math standard -- urgent, district-wide need
- **Middle school math collapse**: Proficiency drops from 35% (Grade 3) to 21% (Grade 7-8) -- the exact intervention window for ModelIt
- **Science proficiency gap**: Only 18.66% meeting science standard (12 pts below state) -- computational modeling addresses both science AND math
- **English Learner lockout**: Only ~5% of ELs meeting math standard -- bilingual ModelIt directly addresses this crisis
- **"Nearly Met" opportunity band**: 27% of students "nearly met" math standard -- these students could be pushed over with modeling-based instruction
- **Positive trajectory**: District is improving (+3 pts/year in math), but still 13 points below state -- they need accelerants, not just more of the same

## Problems They're Solving (from LCAP/Strategic Plan)

1. **Math proficiency crisis**: 77% of students not meeting math standard; 13 points below state average
2. **Middle school math collapse**: Proficiency drops from 35% (Grade 3) to 21% (Grade 7-8) -- pipeline problem before high school
3. **Science proficiency gap**: Only 18.66% meeting science standard; 12 points below state average
4. **English Learner achievement**: Only ~5% of ELs meeting math standard -- bilingual support is critical
5. **Post-ESSER fiscal cliff**: Federal COVID relief funds ($162M total) have expired; $14M projected deficit for 2025-26
6. **Chronic absenteeism**: 22.5% rate (above 20.1% state avg), though improving -7.8 pts year over year
7. **CTE expansion**: $408M Measure I includes robotics, coding, medical, construction lab expansion
8. **STEM facility modernization**: Aging infrastructure needs upgraded instructional technology
9. **College/career readiness**: Expanding pathways, dual enrollment, and AVID across all schools
10. **Teacher pipeline**: CSUSB resident teacher program addresses staffing shortages
