## Why Fontana USD

1. **Critical math gap**: 22.66% math proficiency is 13 points below state average; 50% of students score "Standard Not Met" in math
2. **Middle school math crisis**: Math proficiency drops from 35% in Grade 3 to just 21% by Grade 8 -- the exact grade band where ModelIt intervenes
3. **$408M Measure I bond**: Just passed Nov 2024 for CTE expansion (robotics, coding, medical, construction labs) and STEAM classroom modernization -- ModelIt is the software for those new facilities
4. **86.5% Hispanic + 25% EL**: Bilingual EN/ES feature is a massive differentiator; largest EL-serving district in pipeline
5. **Warm connection through Dr. Alvarez**: Former Fontana USD career educator (Tokay Elementary teacher through Assoc. Supt.) now at neighboring Rialto USD -- can provide introductions to Fontana leadership
6. **22 CTE pathways with STEM alignment**: Engineering, Biotechnology, Mechatronics, Health Science, EMT, Fire Tech -- multiple ModelIt integration points
7. **Discovery Education partnership already active**: District is accustomed to digital STEM platforms; ModelIt complements (not competes with) Discovery Ed
8. **$132M in LCFF Supp/Conc funding**: Massive pool for equity-focused math/science intervention tools targeting 83% unduplicated pupils
