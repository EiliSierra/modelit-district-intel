## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

ModelIt should be positioned as a **computational modeling supplement** that enhances the district's existing hands-on science approach:

1. **Zoo Safari Park connection**: Students observe conservation/ecology at the Safari Park → ModelIt lets them BUILD computational models of those ecosystems (predator-prey, population dynamics, habitat loss)
2. **SAGE Garden connection**: Students grow food and study ecology → ModelIt lets them model plant growth, water cycles, nutrient flows as systems
3. **STEM class integration**: MS STEM teachers already teach dedicated STEM periods → ModelIt fits directly into existing class time
4. **Math + Science bridge**: District is adopting new math curriculum → ModelIt bridges computational thinking and science modeling

### How ModelIt Complements Current Tools

| Current Tool/Program | What It Does | What ModelIt Adds |
