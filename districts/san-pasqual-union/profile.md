1. **Math proficiency gap**: While above state average, 54.6% of students still not meeting math standards
2. **Mathematics curriculum adoption**: April 2025 agenda item suggests math curriculum is being evaluated/refreshed
3. **Technology funding**: Saints Soirée fundraiser specifically targets tech needs not covered by regular funding
4. **Small district resource constraints**: Single school must provide comprehensive K-8 programming
5. **English Learner support**: 55 EL students need specialized instruction (Amanda Grano serves as EL Coordinator)
