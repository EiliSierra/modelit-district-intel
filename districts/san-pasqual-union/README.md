## Why San Pasqual Union

1. **Above-average but with room to grow**: 45% math proficiency is above state avg, but 55% of students still not meeting standard
2. **Dedicated STEM teachers**: Two MS STEM teachers (Lynch, Cutchin) - existing champions who understand modeling
3. **San Diego Zoo Safari Park partnership**: Applied science/conservation curriculum - ModelIt complements with systems modeling
4. **Two science labs**: Facilities ready for computational modeling activities
5. **Small district = fast decisions**: Superintendent/Principal is one person (Burroughs) - single decision maker
6. **Closest to home**: Escondido/San Pasqual Valley, ~15 min from Carlsbad
7. **SAGE Garden + Environmental focus**: Systems thinking already embedded in school culture
