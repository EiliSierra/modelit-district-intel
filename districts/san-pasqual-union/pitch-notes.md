## Key Talking Points

1. **"Build, don't just interact"**: "Most science simulations let students change variables in a pre-built model. ModelIt lets students BUILD the model from scratch. That's a fundamentally different cognitive task - it develops computational thinking, not just observation skills."

2. **"Safari Park to systems models"**: "Your students already observe real ecosystems at the San Diego Zoo Safari Park. ModelIt is the natural next step - they can model those same ecosystems computationally. Predator-prey dynamics, population growth, habitat loss. It turns a field trip into a data-driven investigation."

3. **"NGSS Practice 2 + Practice 5"**: "NGSS calls out 'Developing and Using Models' and 'Using Mathematics and Computational Thinking' as two of the eight Science and Engineering Practices. ModelIt is specifically designed to address both simultaneously."

4. **"Bilingual by design"**: "ModelIt is fully bilingual (English/Spanish). With 37% Hispanic students and 55 English Learners at SPUSD, every student can build models in their preferred language."

5. **"Small school, big impact"**: "In a school where the superintendent, principal, and instructional leader are all the same person, a tool like ModelIt can be implemented quickly and consistently. No bureaucratic layers - just a direct decision that benefits students."

6. **"Math proficiency bridge"**: "45% of SPUSD students meet math standards - above state average, but still 55% not there yet. ModelIt applies mathematical reasoning to real-world problems. Students who struggle with abstract math concepts often succeed when they can see the math working in a model they built."

7. **"15 minutes from Carlsbad"**: "We're your neighbors. We can provide the kind of hands-on support that a national vendor simply can't. Monthly check-ins, in-person PD, classroom observation - whatever your teachers need."

8. **"Foundation-fundable"**: "Your SP School Foundation raises money specifically for technology and curriculum. A ModelIt pilot fits perfectly within that mission - it's a STEM tool that traditional funding doesn't typically cover."

ModelIt adds the computational layer - students model what they observe. It's not 'instead of' the Zoo, it's 'because of' the Zoo. The partnership makes ModelIt MORE relevant, not less." |
| 7 | "I'm not sure our students are ready for computational modeling." | "ModelIt is designed for grades 5-12, and your students are already doing STEM exploratory classes.
