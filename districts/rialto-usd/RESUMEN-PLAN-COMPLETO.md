## La Crisis Academica (Nuestro Argumento de Venta)

### Matematicas - EL PROBLEMA PRINCIPAL
| Grado | % Proficiente | Tendencia |
|-------|--------------|-----------|
| Grado 3 | 33.91% | Punto mas alto |
| Grado 4 | 27.79% | Baja fuerte |
| Grado 5 | 20.62% | Sigue bajando |
| Grado 6 | 22.84% | Leve recuperacion |
| Grado 7 | 20.49% | Baja de nuevo |
| Grado 8 | 17.61% | **PUNTO MAS BAJO** |

**La caida de 34% a 18% entre grados 3 y 8 es nuestro argumento principal.** Ese es exactamente el rango de grados donde ModelIt tiene mayor impacto.

### Por Subgrupo (Matematicas 2025)
- Hispanos: 23.82% (mejorando pero muy bajo)
- Afroamericanos: 13.29% (estancado)
- English Learners: **7.87%** (criticamente bajo)
- Promedio del distrito: 23.73% (10 puntos debajo del estado)

### Ciencias
- Solo ~15% proficiente (14 puntos debajo del estado)

## Programas STEM Existentes (Donde Nos Conectamos)

### STEM CARES
- "Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics Cultivates Active Responsible Environmental Stewards"
- Enfoque: Problemas ambientales locales usando STEM
- **Conexion con ModelIt**: Estudiantes pueden MODELAR los sistemas ambientales que ya investigan (calidad del agua, contaminacion del aire, ecosistemas)

### Herramientas Actuales
| Herramienta | Uso | Nuestra posicion |
|-------------|-----|------------------|
| Discovery Education | Contenido de ciencias K-8 | "ModelIt complementa a DE, no lo reemplaza" |
| Mystery Science | Lecciones elementales | "Despues de la leccion, modelan el sistema" |
| iReady | Diagnostico/intervencion math | "iReady mide gaps, ModelIt construye razonamiento aplicado" |
| Amazon/BootUp | CS en elementales | Diferente espacio - no competimos |

### Fuentes de Presupuesto Identificadas
1. **Title IV-A** (STEM / Well-Rounded Education) - $100K-500K disponible
2. **LCFF Supplemental/Concentration** - Alto por el 59.3% desfavorecido
3. **Title II** - Si se posiciona como PD incluido
4. **Piloto**: $5K-15K para 1-3 escuelas (aprobacion rapida sin board vote)

# PARTE 4: TALKING POINTS PARA LLAMADAS/REUNIONES

## 1. Math Proficiency Gap
"Your math proficiency is at 23.73% -- about 10 points below the state average. But here's what's interesting: it drops from 34% in grade 3 to just 18% by grade 8. That grade 5-8 window is exactly where computational modeling has the biggest impact."

## 2. STEM CARES Alignment
"Your students already investigate local environmental issues through STEM CARES. ModelIt gives them the tools to build computer models of those issues -- simulate water quality, model air pollution dispersion, test ecosystem interventions -- before they act."

## 3. NGSS Practice Alignment
"NGSS Practice 2 is 'Developing and Using Models.' Practice 5 is 'Using Mathematics and Computational Thinking.' ModelIt directly addresses both -- and these are the two hardest practices to implement with existing platforms."

## 4. Equity y Diseno Accesible
"With 86% of your students being Hispanic and a significant English Learner population, the visual nature of computational modeling helps bridge language gaps. Students build and test models visually -- the interface is intuitive regardless of reading level."

## 5. Complementa, No Reemplaza
"You've invested in Discovery Education and Mystery Science, and those are great platforms. ModelIt doesn't replace them -- it extends them. Students watch a DE video about ecosystems, then build a model to test the concepts."

## 6. Credibilidad NSF SBIR
"ModelIt is funded by the National Science Foundation through their SBIR program. It's been through rigorous scientific review. We're not a startup selling an unproven product -- we're a research-backed platform with NSF credibility."

## 7. Costo e Implementacion
"A pilot for 2-3 schools can start at $5K-15K including teacher PD. This fits within Title IV-A or supplemental/concentration funding. We include professional development."
