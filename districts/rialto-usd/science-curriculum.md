## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

**ModelIt does NOT compete with their current stack.** Here's why:

| Current Tool | What It Does | ModelIt Complement |
|-------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Discovery Education | Content library, videos, readings | ModelIt adds computational modeling layer on top of DE content |
| Mystery Science | Guided inquiry lessons with videos | ModelIt extends inquiry into systems modeling after Mystery Science intro |
| PBLworks | Project-based learning framework | ModelIt provides the modeling tool for PBL science projects |
| iReady (math) | Math diagnostic and practice | ModelIt builds mathematical reasoning through modeling applications |

**Key message**: "ModelIt works alongside Discovery Education and Mystery Science - students explore phenomena with those tools, then build and test computational models to deepen understanding."

### Adoption Cycle Opportunity

California's science instructional materials adoption cycle:
- State Board reviews science materials on an 8-year cycle
- Districts can adopt any time but state-adopted materials get preferred pricing
- The next state science adoption is TBD (last was 2018 for NGSS-aligned materials)
- **Between adoption cycles** is when supplemental tools like ModelIt get purchased most easily - no big formal adoption process required

### Grade Band Priority

Based on math proficiency data (where computational modeling has highest impact):

| Grade Band | Math Proficiency | Priority | Rationale |
|-----------|-----------------|----------|-----------|
| 5-8 | 17-21% | **HIGHEST** | Steepest decline in math proficiency; NGSS science modeling standards; CAST testing in grade 8 |
| 3-5 | 21-34% | HIGH | Early intervention; build modeling foundations |
| 9-12 | 23% | MEDIUM | CTE integration, AP prep, college readiness |

## Competitors in This Space

| Competitor | Presence at Rialto | How ModelIt Differs |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Discovery Education | **Active** - primary platform | ModelIt adds computational modeling; DE is content delivery |
| Amplify Science | **Not adopted** | Amplify is full curriculum; ModelIt is supplemental tool |
| FOSS (Full Option Science System) | **Not adopted** | FOSS is kit-based; ModelIt is digital modeling |
| STEMscopes | **Not adopted** | STEMscopes is full NGSS curriculum; ModelIt supplements |
| OpenSciEd | **Not adopted** | OpenSciEd is free OER curriculum; ModelIt adds modeling |
| PhET Simulations | **Unknown** | PhET has pre-built sims; ModelIt lets students BUILD models |
| StarLogo Nova | **Unknown** | Agent-based modeling; ModelIt focuses on systems dynamics |
| NetLogo | **Unknown** | Similar space but complex; ModelIt is more accessible K-12 |

**Best positioning**: ModelIt is the only tool that lets students build their own computational models of scientific phenomena, directly addressing NGSS Practice 2 (Developing and Using Models) and Practice 5 (Using Mathematics and Computational Thinking).
