## STEM Landscape

### STEM CARES Program
- **Full name**: Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics Cultivates Active Responsible Environmental Stewards
- **Focus**: Environmental stewardship, green school ideals, living schoolyards
- **Type**: Grassroots student organization using locally relevant environmental issues
- **Approach**: Think globally, act locally - community improvement through STEM
- **Contact**: STEM team at (909) 879-6002 Ext. 2977, 324 N. Palm Ave, Rialto, CA 92376
- **ModelIt fit**: Systems modeling of ecosystems, pollution, water cycles perfectly aligns

### Amazon Future Engineer / BootUp Partnership
- **Scope**: 15 elementary schools, 7,000+ students
- **Provider**: BootUp PD (nonprofit CS education specialist)
- **Funding**: Amazon Future Engineer program
- **Focus**: Elementary computer science - coding, robotics, project-based learning
- **Status**: Active as of 2025

### MESA (Mathematics, Engineering, Science Achievement)
- **Location**: Rialto High School confirmed
- **Type**: Pre-college academic preparation program
- **Focus**: Engineering, science, and math for underrepresented students

### Science & Engineering Fair
- **Requirement**: Mandatory K-5 participation
- **Scope**: Annual district-wide event

### Environmental Literacy
- **Framework**: California Environmental Principles and Concepts integrated into curriculum
- **Partners**: Ten Strands (featured Rialto as environmental literacy model)
- **Recognition**: CDE Green Ribbon / ED-GRS recognition

## LCAP Budget Summary

*Note: Detailed line items in [lcap-extract.md](lcap-extract.md). The following is high-level summary from available data.*

### Funding Structure
- **LCFF Base Grant**: Primary operational funding
- **Supplemental/Concentration**: High allocation due to 59.3% economically disadvantaged + high EL population
- **Title I**: Schoolwide eligible (most schools qualify)
- **Title II**: Teacher quality/PD
- **Title IV**: STEM/technology/well-rounded education
- **Bonds/Local**: Capital improvements, technology refresh

### Known STEM-Adjacent Spending Areas
1. Science curriculum (Discovery Education, Mystery Science licenses)
2. Math intervention (iReady diagnostic/instruction)
3. STEM CARES program operations
4. CTE pathway materials and certifications
5. Amazon/BootUp CS program (externally funded)
6. Technology (1:1 device program maintenance)
7. Professional development (NGSS implementation, science coaching)

## Problems They're Solving (from LCAP/Public Data)

1. **Math proficiency crisis**: Only 23.73% meet standards (10+ pts below state)
2. **Science proficiency gap**: ~15% meet standards (14 pts below state)
3. **English Learner achievement**: 7.87% math proficiency, 12.73% ELA
4. **Grade 3-8 math decline**: Proficiency drops from 34% to 18% across elementary/middle
5. **African American achievement**: 13.29% math proficiency - persistent gap
6. **College/career readiness**: Need to increase A-G completion and CCI rates
7. **Environmental stewardship**: Community-based STEM through STEM CARES
8. **Digital literacy**: CS education expanding through Amazon partnership
9. **Leadership transition**: New superintendent (Dec 2025) establishing priorities
