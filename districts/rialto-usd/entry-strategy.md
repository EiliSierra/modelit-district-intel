## Decision Process

1. **Staff evaluation** - Chan-Roden and team review the tool, see demo, assess NGSS alignment
2. **Pilot approval** - Small pilot can be approved at director/associate superintendent level
3. **Budget allocation** - Must fit within existing LCAP action or be proposed as new action for next cycle
4. **Board approval** - Contracts over threshold amount require board vote (usually $50K+)
5. **Implementation** - PD training, teacher onboarding, student rollout

**Fast track**: A small pilot (1-3 schools, single grade band) can often be approved by Education Services leadership without going to the full board, using existing discretionary or Title IV funds.
