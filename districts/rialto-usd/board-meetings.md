## Notes on Data Collection

Board agendas are published as PDF documents on the district website. Full extraction requires downloading and reading each PDF agenda/minutes. The links below are documented for systematic extraction. Items marked with [NEEDS PDF REVIEW] require downloading the actual agenda document.

**Focus areas for extraction**:
- STEM/STEAM/science/math spending approvals
- CTE pathway expansions or new programs
- Technology purchases (devices, software, platforms)
- Science curriculum adoptions (publisher, program, grade levels, contract value)
- Other curriculum adoptions
- Professional development contracts
- Partnership agreements (universities, nonprofits, industry)
- Consultant/vendor contracts in education services
- Budget amendments and mid-year adjustments
- Grant acceptances

- **Action**: Board unanimously selected Dr. Alejandro Alvarez as Superintendent
- **Date**: Board selection ~Oct 28, 2025; contract approved ~Nov 13, 2025
- **Amount**: $356,000/year salary
- **Source**: [IECN](https://iecn.com/rialto-unified-board-dr-alejandro-alvarez-superintendent/), [SB Sun](https://www.sbsun.com/2025/11/13/rialto-unified-school-board-officially-hires-new-superintendent/)
- **Context**: Hired amid forensic audit of district finances

- **Partner**: Amazon + BootUp PD
- **Impact**: 7,000+ students
- **Source**: [IECN](https://iecn.com/amazon-press-release-final-docx/)
