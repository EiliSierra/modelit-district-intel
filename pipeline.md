## Pipeline Stages

| Stage | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 0 - Research | Gathering district intelligence |
| 1 - Identified | Research complete, entry strategy defined |
| 2 - Contacted | Initial outreach sent |
| 3 - Engaged | Call/meeting scheduled or completed |
| 4 - Demo | Hands-on demo delivered |
| 5 - Pilot Proposed | Written pilot proposal submitted |
| 6 - Pilot Active | Pilot underway |
| 7 - Evaluation | Pilot results being reviewed |
| 8 - Expansion | District-wide adoption in progress |
| 9 - Won | Contract signed |

## Target District Criteria

When selecting new districts for analysis, prioritize:

1. **Math proficiency below state average** (< 34%) - bigger gap = bigger need
2. **High Hispanic/Latino enrollment** (> 50%) - visual/intuitive interface benefits EL students
3. **Active STEM programs** - existing champions who understand the space
4. **LCAP cycle alignment** - Jan-June development window for next year
5. **Geographic proximity** - San Diego, Inland Empire, LA County first (for in-person demos)
6. **District size** - 10,000-50,000 students (big enough to matter, small enough to navigate)
7. **Board STEM advocates** - look for board members with STEM backgrounds or vocal support
